Lady Gaga is very grateful for where she is at in life. “I’m in a new place,” the actress-musician says in a new interview. “I've been in the music industry since I was a teenager. And, you know, I think that I just went through a lot.” She adds, “I just feel a lot better now, and it's nice to be able to say that. I feel really grateful to be able to say that.” Source: People
