Taylor Swift Is Life is a new unofficial fan book that joins the growing list of Swift-themed books for fans to display on your coffee table. Not only does it show off an adorable illustrated title, it also features everything a fan could possibly want to know about the “Fortnight” star including a biography of her life, “Swiftology” terms that covers significant words, imagery and people in the popstar’s life, according to the official description.

	The latest book officially dropped on Tuesday (July 16) and you can find copies for as little as $20 on Target, Amazon and Walmart. 

	Keep reading to pick up a copy of the book now.

		Already, the superfan guide has landed itself a No. 1 new release on Amazon for pop artist biographies. Readers will find everything from her entire discography (up to her album Midnights) and notes on the significance of each song and lyric. Astrology lovers can also become enchanted over your own personal Swiftscope, which will tell you which of her songs most aligns with your sign as well as how your star aligns with Swift’s music.	

	Author Kathleen Perricone has experience freelancing for long-form magazines and was even a celebrity news editor in a previous life. The “Cruel Summer” singer isn’t the only musician she’s covered either, this book joins her series Modern Icons, which also includes upcoming books of similar styles for Beyoncé, Harry Styles, Lady Gaga and Rihanna. The upcoming books are expected to drop throughout the rest of 2024 through 2025, but you can preorder copies now.
