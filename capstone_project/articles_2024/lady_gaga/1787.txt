Beyonce’s new single “Texas Hold ‘Em” has officially become her ninth solo hit to top the Billboard Hot 100 chart. The country-crossover earned 29 million streams, 29,000 sales, and 16 million radio impressions. Queen Bey became the first Black woman in modern music history to top Billboard’s Hot Country Songs chart last week, and “Texas Hold ‘Em” remains No. 1 on the new chart.
She dethroned Jack Harlow’s “Lovin On Me” to take the top spot, sending it down to No. 2 this week. Teddy Swims’ “Lose Control” rises to No. 3, Kanye West and Ty Dolla $ign’s “Carnival” slips to No. 4, and Benson Boone’s “Beautiful Things” rounds out the top 5.
