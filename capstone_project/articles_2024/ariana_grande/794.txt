Coldplay were the musical guests on the newest episode of Saturday Night Live. On the NBC program, hosted by comedian Nate Bargatze, they played two songs from their newly released album, Moon Music. Watch Coldplay do “All My Love” and “We Pray,” with Elyanna and Tini, below.
The 50th season of Saturday Night Live kicked off on September 28 with musical guest Jelly Roll and host Jean Smart. The next episode of Saturday Night Live will feature host Ariana Grande and musical guest Stevie Nicks.
