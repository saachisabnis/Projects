With its handsome and diverse cast, a soundtrack including reworked Ariana Grande and Taylor Swift songs and steamy sex scenes, the Netflix show Bridgerton has brought historical drama to a whole new audience since its debut in 2021.
Such is the appetite for the Regency-era drama, the first four episodes of series three hit 45.1 million views in its opening weekend earlier this month. 
The popularity of Bridgerton, as well as other period dramas such as The Crown and Outlander, has led to a resurgence of interest in visiting English stately homes and other locations depicted in the shows.
