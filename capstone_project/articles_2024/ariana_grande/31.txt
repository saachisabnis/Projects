This week Universal Studios announced that they are bringing "Wicked" themed experiences to their parks in Hollywood and Orlando in October.
According to KTLA 5, inside Universal Studios Hollywood Feature Presentation retail store they "will showcase props from the film, an exciting photo op will debut within the theme park and, later in tandem with the film opening, costumes will be displayed within the Universal Cinema, an AMC Theatre, at Universal CityWalk,” a news release said.
Aside from this showcase of props there will be merch that includes a collection from Shizz University. Guests can also make their way to CityWalk to check out treats inspired by the film at Voodoo Doughnuts and The Toothsome Chocolate Emporium & Savory Feast Kitchen. "Wicked" will be released in theaters on November 22 and will star Ariana Grande, Cynthia Erivo, Jonathan Bailey, Michelle Yeoh, Jeff Goldblum, and more!
