This is Beyoncé's third Number 1 on the Sky VIP Big Top 40! 
Beyoncé has landed at Number 1 with her surprise country single 'TEXAS HOLD 'EM'!
The singer dropped this track and Number 14's '16 Carriages' following the Grammy Awards this month, and the songs have flown to the top of the charts.
This marks Bey's third Number 1 - with Perfect (with Ed Sheeran) and 2022's Break My Soul.
The Top 4 songs from this week are all new entries - Number 4 is Ariana Grande and Mariah Carey's yes, and?, Number 3 is Calvin Harris & Rag 'N' Bone Man's Lovers in a Past Life, and Number 2 is Dua Lipa's hit Training Season.
The biggest climber this week was Nathan Dawe's We Ain't Here For Long, and this week's biggest faller is Ariana Grande's yes, and?
Super Bowl performer Usher has two songs on this week's Big Top 40 - Kissing Strangers and Yeah!.
