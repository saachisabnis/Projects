Believe it or not, "Saturday Night Live" is already at its 50th season. It premieres on the 28th with host Jean Smart and musical guest Jelly Roll.
The lineup for the four following shows was also announced. Here's a breakdown of the schedule so far:
1. September 28th: Jean Smart, musical guest Jelly Roll
2. October 5th: Comedian Nate Bargatze, musical guest Coldplay
3. October 12th:  Ariana Grande, musical guest Stevie Nicks (Her first time back since 1983!)
4. October 19th:  Michael Keaton, musical guest Billie Eilish
5. November 2nd:  John Mulaney, musical guest Chappell Roan
"SNL" will also celebrate its 50th anniversary with a three-hour primetime special on February 16th, 2025.
In related news, "Saturday Night" hits theaters October 11th. That's the movie about the show's first episode, which premiered October 11th, 1975.
