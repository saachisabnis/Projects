Ariana Grande’s new single, “The Boy Is Mine,” reimagines the Grammy-winning 1998 hit collaboration by Brandy and Monica. And now Grande brings her song full circle, by announcing that an upcoming remix of “The Boy Is Mine” will feature the legendary singers Brandy and Monica. The remix of “The Boy Is Mine” will drop on June 21, and Grande is psyched to work with her musical heroes. 
”I cannot believe this is real,” Grande posted on Instagram. “My deepest and sincerest thank you to Brandy and Monica, not only for joining me for this moment, but for your generosity, your kindness, and for the countless ways in which you have inspired me. it is near impossible to say how much this means to me. this is in celebration of you both and the impact that you have had on every vocalist, vocal producer, musician, artist that is creating today. i loooooove you both so so much.” (People)
