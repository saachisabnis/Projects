During an intimate performance on Monday, Keith Urban played an acoustic cover of Ariana Grande’s hit single, “We Can’t Be Friends.” Urban gushed about his love of the song while sharing a clip of his performance on Instagram, captioning his post, “This song is audible heroin.” Grande responded in the comments, “this is so beautiful ???? & thank youuuuu !” During a conversation with People, Urban praised Ariana’s catchy, infectious tune, “That’s my absolute current obsession song right now. I don’t know what is going on with that song, but I cannot stop playing it. I play that thing over and over and over.” 
“We Can’t Be Friends” was the second single lifted from Grande’s recent record, Eternal Sunshine. It became Grande’s sixth No. 1 album in her career, while “We Can’t Be Friends” went on to top the Billboard Hot 100 singles chart as well. (Variety)
