Jack Harlow’s “Lovin On Me” returned to No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 for its fourth non-consecutive week. It replaced Ariana Grande’s “Yes, And?,” which fell to No. 6 a week after becoming her eighth No. 1 hit. Taylor Swift’s “Cruel Summer” was back up to No. 2 this week, Tate McRae’s “Greedy” was back at No. 3. Teddy Swims’ “Lose Control” follows at No. 4, two weeks after becoming his first top 10 hit. Zach Bryan’s “I Remember Everything,” featuring Kacey Musgraves, rounds out the top 5. Doja Cat has both No. 7 (“Agora Hills”) and No. 8 (“Paint The Town Red”), followed by SZA’s “Snooze” at No. 9 and 21 Savage’s “Redrum” in the No. 10 spot.
