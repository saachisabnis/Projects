Country music gears up for a special night as the 2024 People’s Choice Country Awards prepare to take over the Grand Ole Opry for a two-hour event. Airing on both NBC and Peacock, the awards will take place at the historic venue on September 26 at 8:00 p.m. ET/PT. And besides looking to honor some of the biggest stars in the genre, the awards will be hosted by the iconic Shania Twain. Excited to take the stage, the night is sure to come packed with memorable performances and more than enough history. With the nominations released, it seems that stars like Zach Bryan, Kacey Musgraves, and Beyonce are leading double-digit nominations. 
As the name suggests, the 2024 People’s Choice Country Awards are voted on by the fans. And given that fans are the ones who make up the genre, their votes matter a great deal. Looking at Beyonce, she leads the nominations with 12.

Don’t miss the 2024 People’s Choice Country Awards, airing September 26 at 8:00 p.m. ET/PT on NBC and Peacock. Hosting the event, Twain shared her excitement, stating, “I am honored to host an award show that celebrates these awesome fans. Get ready for an unforgettable show with lots of big hair, glamour, rhinestones, hats, boots and incredible performances. Giddy Up!”
