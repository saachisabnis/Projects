The Libertines are Peter Doherty – vocals/guitar, Carl Barât – vocals/guitar, John Hassall – bass guitar and Gary Powell– drums.
Noughties indie band The Libertines has announced a headline tour across the UK & Ireland later this year with a dates in Belfast and Dublin. 
The band, fronted by Pete Doherty, is set to play at the Belfast Telegraph Building on September 24 a day after taking to the stage in Dublin’s 3 Olympia.
