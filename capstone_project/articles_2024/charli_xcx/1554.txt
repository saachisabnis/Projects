Sabrina Carpenter will headline BST Hyde Park festival next summer, it has been announced.
The US pop star is set to perform her UK number one songs Espresso, Taste and Please, Please, Please alongside her catalogue of hits on July 5 2025, with special musical guests Clairo and Beabadoobee.
The 25-year-old joins previously announced headliners including actor Hugh Jackman, country star Zach Bryan and Jeff Lynne’s ELO, alongside pop singers Olivia Rodrigo and Noah Kahan on the bill.
Sabrina Carpenter will headline BST (BST)

Disco bop Espresso, which spent seven weeks at the top of the UK charts, secured Grammy nominations for record of the year and best pop solo performance.
It was also named the biggest song of 2024 by BBC Radio 1, beating the likes of Charli XCX and Chappell Roan to the title.
Meanwhile, Carpenter is also competing for best new artist at the upcoming Grammys, while Please Please Please has been nominated for song of the year and her album Short N’ Sweet received an album of the year and best pop vocal album nod.
This year’s festival saw headline performances from the likes of Fleetwood Mac star Stevie Nicks, R&B singer SZA, pop artist Robbie Williams and country pop star Shania Twain.
General sale for tickets begin at 10am on Wednesday December 11.
