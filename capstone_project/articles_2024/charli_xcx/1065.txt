Kasabian were a surprise act at the Glastonbury Festival in June. (Ben Birchall/PA)
Kasabian frontman Sergio Pizzorno has hailed the band’s fans as the “greatest in the world” following them earning a seventh number one album.
The Leicester rockers topped the charts this week with their latest record Happenings – bringing them level with Blur, Muse, The Prodigy, Sir Paul McCartney, Barbra Streisand, Sir Elton John, Sir Cliff Richard and Ed Sheeran.
