Taylor Swift has officially made her debut on Forbes‘ annual billionaires list with an estimated personal fortune of $1.1 billion.
She’s the first musician in history to achieve billionaire status solely based on her music and live performances., though her real estate portfolio is also a major contributor.
The Eras Tour has generated over $1 billion in revenue since its launch in March 2023, making it a significant contributor to her wealth.
Additionally, her concert film from the tour has become the highest-grossing concert film of all time, earning over $261 million at the box office.
