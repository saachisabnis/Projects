As the celebrity guests including Bill Gates, Ivanka Trump and Mark Zuckerberg descended from their private jets, they were welcomed with garlands of white flowers and folk dancers before what has been billed as the biggest party a small Indian city has even seen.
Industry titans, politicians and pop stars arrived on Friday in Jamnagar, Gujarat, for the start of a three-day pre-wedding celebration hosted by the billionaire tycoon Mukesh Ambani for his younger son, Anant, and his soon-to-be wife, Radhika Merchant.
With a net worth of some $116 billion, Mukesh Ambani, 66, is India’s richest man and the ninth richest man in the world. He is chairman of Reliance Industries, and its refinery at Jamnagar is the largest in the world.
