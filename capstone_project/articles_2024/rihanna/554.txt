Today we celebrate not just a legendary rapper, but also a devoted father, husband, entrepreneur, and cultural icon. Born Clifford Joseph Harris Jr. on September 25, 1980, T.I. rose from the streets of Atlanta to become one of hip-hop’s most influential figures. As a musician, he pioneered the trap music genre, blessing the world with hits like “Whatever You Like,” “Live Your Life,” and “Rubberband Man.”
