SALEM Ore. (KPTV) - The Oregon Department of Human Services (ODHS) says a teenager who went missing in June has been found.
Nevaeh “Rihanna” Rohrbach, 15, went missing from the Clackamas area on June 21. She was found Aug. 16
Oregon DHS did not provide any other details.
