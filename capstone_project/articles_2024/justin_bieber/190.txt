The Voice UK is back for 2024 along with a new look judges/coaches panel.


  Sir Tom Jones and Will.i.am are back for another season, but will be joined by three new faces for the latest series of the singing talent show.


  The 2024 series will see a The Voice UK first with a double chair occupied by McFly band members Tom Fletcher and Danny Jones.


  While LeAnn Rimes - who is known for songs including How Do I Live Without You and Can't Fight the Moonlight from the Coyote Ugly movie - will also join the judges/coaches panel in 2024.


  Rimes, Fletcher and Jones replace Anne-Marie and Olly Murs from last years panel.


  Emma Willis will also return as host of the The Voice UK in 2024.


  Will.i.am, or William Adams, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, actor, and entrepreneur.


  He is the frontman of the Black Eyed Peas which has produced hit songs including the likes of Where is the Love, Pump It and I Got a Feeling.


  Will.i.am has also had a successful solo career producing hits including #thatpower featuring Justin Bieber, Scream & Shout with Britney Spears and T.H.E. (The Hardest Ever) which featured Mick Jagger and Jennifer Lopez.


  He has also appeared in several movies including X-Men Origins: Wolverine and leant his voice to characters in animated movies Rio (1 and 2) and Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa.


  Will.i.am's net worth is estimated to be around USD$50 million (roughly £3.8 million), according to Celebrity Net Worth.


McFly's Tom Fletcher (far left) and Danny Jones (far right) along with LeAnn Rimes (middle) will be joining The Voice UK in 2024. (Image: ITV)


  Sir Tom Jones (officially Sir Thomas John Woodward OBE) is a Welsh singer who began his music career back in the 60s.


  His is known worldwide for hits including the likes of It's Not Unusual, Sex Bomb, Delilah and What's New Pussycat.


  Throughout his career he has sold more than 100 million records, while also enjoying success in the charts, boasting 36 Top 40 hits in the UK and 19 in the US. 


  It is estimated Sir Tom's net worth is USD$300 million (roughly £228 million), according to Celebrity Net Worth.


Danny Jones and Tom Fletcher are members of pop band McFly along with Harry Judd and Dougie Poynter.


  McFly is known for songs including All About Her, Five Colours In Her Hair and Obviously along with a number of other hits.


  They even featured in the 2006 movie Just My Luck with Lindsay Lohan and Chris Pine.


  Jones won the most recent series of The Masked Singer UK earlier this year dressed as Piranha. 


    🎉🎤🏆 BIG FINS UP for the WINNER of the #MaskedSingerUK 2024 - our FIN-tastic CHAMPION #PIRANHA aka @itsDannyJones! 🎶🌟 👑 pic.twitter.com/7oRCoYZcOy


  Jones is married to former Miss England Georgia Horsley and together the pair have one child - Cooper.


  As for Fletcher, he is not only the founding member of McFly but is also an author having released several books including There's a Monster in Your Book, The Christmasaurus and the Naughty List, The Creakers and Eve of Man which he wrote with his wife Giovanna.


  He is married to Giovanna Fletcher and the pair have three children - Buzz, Buddy and Max.


  Jones' estimated net worth is between £2 and £4 million, according to The Express, while Fletcher's is thought to be USD$5 Million (£3.8 million), according to Celebrity Net Worth.


  LeAnn Rimes is an American singer/songwriter known for hit songs including How Do I Live Without You and Can't Fight the Moonlight.


  Rimes, 42, has enjoyed a highly decorated music career since enjoying her first taste of success at the early age of 13 as a country singer. 


  Since then she has gone on to sell more than 40 million records worldwide while winning several awards including Grammy and Billboard Music Awards.


  It is estimated her net worth is around USD$10 Million (£7.6 million), according to Celebrity Net Worth.


  Emma Willis, according to Celebrity Net Worth, is estimated to have a net worth of around USD$8 Million (£6 million).


  The Voice host has held various TV and radio presenting roles throughout her career across networks including the likes of Channel 5, BBC, ITV and Heart FM.

  She has appeared as host of other ITV shows including Big Brother and This Morning, while she also has her own Sunday morning radio show on Heart FM with Stephen Mulhern.


  She has also recently appeared as host, alongside husband Matt, of the UK version of Love is Blind on Netflix.


The first episode of The Voice UK 2024 airs tonight (Saturday, August 31) on ITV1, ITVX and STV at 8pm.
