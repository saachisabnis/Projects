The phenomenon that is Kendrick Lamar’s “Not Like Us” officially secures #1 on this week’s Mediabase rhythmic radio chart.
Played ~6,416 times during the June 2-8 tracking period, “Not Like Us” rises two spots to #1. This week’s spin count tops last week’s mark by 1,141.
Up two places, Cardi B’s “Enough (Miami)” secures #2.
Future, Metro Boomin & Kendrick Lamar’s “Like That” descends one spot to #3, while Tommy Richman’s “Million Dollar Baby” jumps three places to #4. A one-place rise concurrently brings Bryson Tiller’s “Whatever She Wants” to #5.
