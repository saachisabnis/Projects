Chris Stapleton has signed on to perform at The Fillmore New Orleans. The concert will occur on Thursday, February 6, 2025. It will function as a kick-off party to one of football’s biggest weekends, the Super Bowl LIX, held on Sunday, February 9, at Caesars Superdome in New Orleans and streamed via the Fox network at 6:30 p.m. E.T. 
According to SiriusXM and Pandora, who have teamed up to sponsor the live show, the 10-time Grammy Award winner will lean into music from this discography, five studio records, and countless singles, including his latest, highly acclaimed 2023 contribution, Higher.
For those unable to attend the concert in person, streaming will be available through Sirius XM Chris Stapleton, channel 63, and the Chris Stapleton Radio station on Pandora. 
Previously, it was announced that Kendrick Lamar would assume the role of headliner for the 2025 edition of the Super Bowl Halftime Show, adding another musical layer to the sports-filled weekend.
