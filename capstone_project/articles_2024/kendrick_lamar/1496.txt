BG Knocc Out discussed the dynamics of the beef between Drake and Kendrick Lamar, asserting that the conflict effectively ended after an incident involving Drake’s bodyguard. The conversation touched on the speculation that the shooting was tied to Drake’s earlier disses of Toronto artists, likely making the situation too 'real' for Drake. BG Knocc Out, an established figure in Compton's music scene, emphasized that Kendrick Lamar, being from Compton, is less inclined to engage in such disputes, adhering to a local culture of focusing on personal matters and avoiding unnecessary conflict.BG Knocc Out praised Kendrick’s artistry, highlighting his creativity and consistency from his mixtape days to his current success. He noted Kendrick's strategic entry into mainstream music, where he initially catered to popular tastes before fully showcasing his unique style. Reflecting on the cultural impact of Compton and iconic diss tracks, BG Knocc Out placed Kendrick’s work among top industry standards, stressing the long-lasting resonance of music that speaks authentically to one's origins.
