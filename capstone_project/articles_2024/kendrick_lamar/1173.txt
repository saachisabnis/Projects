	See below for everything you need to know about the 2024 BET Hip Hop Awards, hosted by Fat Joe.

	










			Trending on Billboard		






	This year’s BET Hip Hop Awards will air Tuesday (Oct. 15) at 8 p.m. ET/PT on BET. The show will simulcast on BET HER, VH1 and MTV2.

	Those who want to watch the BET Hip Hop Awards online can tune in on BET.com with a cable provider login.

	No cable? Signing up for free trials of DirecTV, Philo and Fubo, YouTube TV or a subscription to SlingTV will give you access to BET as well, to let you watch the 2024 BET Hip Hop Awards live on TV or stream the BET Hip Hop Awards from your laptop, tablet or phone.

	You can also watch a livestream of BET (live in most markets) plus dozens of other TV channels via Hulu + Live TV, which currently has a rare three-day free trial. Philo offers streaming plans for $25/month after a free trial for a week. Subscribe here.

	Megan Thee Stallion leads this year’s race, earning 12 nods, followed by Kendrick Lamar with 11 nods. Drake landed eight nominations; Cardi B, GloRilla, Metro Boomin and Nicki Minaj landed seven; and Future and Travis Scott are tied at six nominations each. Other nominees include 21 Savage, 41, A$AP Rocky, Common, Lil Wayne, Pete Rock and Sexyy Red.

	You can expect to see performances by 310babii, 2 Chainz, Big Boogie featuring GloRilla, Bossman Dlow, E-40, Juicy J, Soulja Boy featuring Roscoe Dash, and Trina featuring Yung Miami, according to Paramount.

	Travis Scott will be honored with the BET Hip Hop Awards 2024 “I Am Hip Hop Award,” which will be presented to him by Teyana Taylor and Tyla during the broadcast. According to Paramount, the 33-year-old is being celebrated and recognized for his “creative genius, cultural contributions and incredible talent, which have catapulted him to the top of the game as one of the most innovative forces in music and popular culture.”
