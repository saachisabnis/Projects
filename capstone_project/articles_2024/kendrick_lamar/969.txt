Tonight's the night -- Hip-Hop's Biggest Night, to be precise. 
Artists who will take the stage at the BET Hip-Hop Awards tonight (Tuesday) include 2 Chainz, Big Boogie, Bossman Dlow, E-40, GloRilla, Juicy J and Trina. Other names set to perform at Drai’s Beachclub and Nightclub include 310babii, Roscoe Dash, Soulja Boy, and Yung Miami. 
When it comes to awards, Megan Thee Stallion and Kendrick Lamar lead the pack. Meg has 12 nods to her name, while Kendrick trails right behind with 11. Meanwhile, Drake managed to rack up recognition in eight different categories. Other artists with multiple nominations include Cardi B, GloRilla, Metro Boomin and Nicki Minaj, all of whom are in the running in seven different fields, while Future, Travis Scott, and 21 Savage all also received several nominations each.
The event will also feature Travis Scot being honored with the I Am Hip-Hop award.
