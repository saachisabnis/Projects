Yoga
Join the Tamaqua Public Library’s free Taylor Swift inspired yoga program for young people ages 11 to 18.
The program will be held from 11 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Please bring a yoga mat or beach towel.
Call the library at 570-668-4660 or visit the library at 30 S. Railroad St., Tamaqua to register.

Spring program
A free Spring Story & S.T.E.M. Program for children in kindergarten through third grade is being offered by the Tamaqua Public Library.
Sessions will be held from 5:30 to 6:30 p.m. Mondays on April 15; April 22; April 29; May 6; May 13, and May 20.
Call the library or visit to register.
May the fourth be with you
The Tamaqua Public Library will hold Star Wars Day on May 4.
Sessions for children up to age 5 will be held from 10 a.m. to 11 a.m.; for children ages 5 to 10 from 11 a.m. to 12 p.m.; for ages 11 through 17 from 12 p.m. to 1 p.m., and for those age 18 and older from 1 p.m. to 2 p.m.
All three children’s programs will offer a story, a snack, and a craft/ S.T.E.M. Project. The adult session features a trivia contest.
Call the library or visit to register.
