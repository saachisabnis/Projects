EVANSVILLE, Ind. (WFIE) - Listen up, Swifties!
“Taylorville - A Tribute to Taylor Swift” is coming to the Old National Events Plaza on June 23, 2024.
A spokesperson with the event tells us the show will feature stunning video wall production, adding a visual display that complements each song.
“This dynamic tribute band is bringing unmatched musical talent to the stage, taking fans on a journey through Taylor’s iconic career,” says a concert organizer.
Tickets are on sale now. Visit oldnationaleventsplaza.com for more information.
