Officers in Tribeca picked up a man who attempted to get into Taylor Swift’s townhouse on Saturday afternoon, police reported.
Members of the 1st Precinct stopped the suspect while responding to a 911 call regarding a disorderly person along Franklin Street at about 1:35 p.m. on Jan. 20.
Upon arriving at the location, the officers were informed that the unidentified disorderly person attempted to open the door to the townhouse. According to WABC-TV, the townhouse happens to belong to the chart-topping singer. 

Following questioning, police learned that the individual had an open arrest warrant against him for failing to answer a summons in Brooklyn back in 2017, and took him into custody. No other charges have been filed against him at this time, WABC-TV reported.
