LAS VEGAS, Nevada (KLTV) - Calling her “great people,” Kansas City Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes said at a press conference Thursday he can understand why Taylor Swift is getting so much attention this year.
“I think when you watch what Taylor has done in her career and how successful she has been, and then you meet her and realize how down-to-earth and what great people she is, you understand why people are so interested in knowing about her,” Mahomes said.
The Whitehouse native also spoke on a variety of topics at ranging from how in-sync he is with Kansas City Chiefs Coach Andy Reid to the work his team put in to make their way back to the big game. You’ll also hear him say whether he prefers crunchy or soft tacos.
