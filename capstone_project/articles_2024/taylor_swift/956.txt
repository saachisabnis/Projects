It's not unusual in the high-stakes world of country music for singers to end up in court over business disputes, but things turn especially uncomfortable when an artist goes to war against their own record label.
It happens more often than you'd think. Tim McGraw spent literally years at war with his own label, as did Brad Paisley.
Toby Keith, Kenny Rogers, LeAnn Rimes, Taylor Swift, Morgan Wallen and more country singers are also among the stars who fought their own record labels, both in and of court.
