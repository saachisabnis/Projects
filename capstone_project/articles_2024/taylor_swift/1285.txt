The 2024 Grammy Awards red carpet has arrived!
Musicians, artists, producers, songwriters and other music industry professionals arrived on the red carpet at the 66th Annual Grammy Awards tonight (Feb. 4), and the fashion did not disappoint.
The live awards ceremony will take place at the Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles, and music's biggest stars such as Taylor Swift, Dua Lipa, Olivia Rodrigo and more are set to appear.

Although the main telecast doesn't begin until 8 PM ET on CBS, the premiere ceremony begins at 3PM ET and the red carpet started at 6PM ET. The red carpet will air during E!'s red carpet special, on the Grammy website and official Grammy social media accounts.
