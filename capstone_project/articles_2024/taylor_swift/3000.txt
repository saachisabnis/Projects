NEW ULM – Jefferson Elementary’s 3rd graders sang, played and danced to the hits Friday. The annual program is put together by Jefferson’s Music and Phy Ed. Departments. The 3rd grade class sing songs and perform choregoraphed dance numbers. 
The title of this year’s program was “Best Self, Best Work, Best World.” All the songs in the program were based on inspiration messages, such as “Keep Your Head Up” by Andy Grammer; “Shake it Off” by  Taylor Swift and “Roar” by Katy Perry.
