CLEVELAND, Ohio (WOIO) - NFL star and Cleveland Heights native Travis Kelce was spotted grabbing a bite to eat at a restaurant downtown Saturday.
Kelce, who is coming off of his third Super Bowl Championship, stopped by Geraci’s Slice Shop, located on Prospect Avenue.
“Next time, bring Taylor [Swift],” the restaurant joked in their Instagram post.
