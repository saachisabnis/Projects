Travis Kelce can do anything! He had a reality dating show and now he is dating the amazing Taylor Swift! He has won the Superbowl 3 times, he has hosted Saturday Night Live and he has a successful podcast with his brother Jason.
So, what else is there for Travis Kelce? How about game show host?
Travis Kelce is in talks to host a reboot of the tv game show, ” Are you smarter than a 5th grader?” It would stream on Amazon Prime.
Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader?, has adult contestants attempting to answer questions from a fifth-grade textbook. The contestants can also use the help of actual fifth-graders during the game. The original show aired on Fox for four seasons with comedian Jeff Foxworthy as the host from 2007 to 2009.
