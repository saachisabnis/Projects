(CNN) – One of pop culture’s power couples is giving a garment to a good cause.
A Kansas City Chiefs jersey autographed by Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift will be auctioned off for charity.
The jersey also comes with a picture of the NFL tight end and the singer-songwriter.
By Friday night, the price tag was listed at more than $14,000.
The Guelph Wish Fund for children is offering the signed shirt. The Canadian-based organization raises money for children with major illnesses or life-altering injuries.
Bidding, which can be done in person or online, ends Saturday.
