RENO, Nev. (KOLO) - After a sold out event last year, the 2nd annual Lover Fest returns to Cypress Saturday, Feb. 10. This is a Taylor Swift Eras Tour inspired dance party for people 18 and older. 
DJ Funn co-owners and fellow “Swifties,” Daryl Dunn and Joe Hurley, stopped by Morning Break to promote their event and encourage guests to get tickets ASAP.
