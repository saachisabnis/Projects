Chappell Roan is opening up about the advice Elton John gave her as she skyrocketed to fame! 
According to People, in a brand new interview she said, "The advice he gave me was that the songs will come… He thought that he wouldn’t have the ideas, but they were absolutely there. He just had to let them come to him. So that’s a good reminder."
They note this comes just a few days after Elton was names Time’s "Icon of the Year!"
