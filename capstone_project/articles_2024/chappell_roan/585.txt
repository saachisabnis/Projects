Comedian-actor Bill Burr will host the upcoming "Saturday Night Live" episode on Nov. 9, with musical guest Mk.gee. Charli XCX is set to host and perform on the Nov. 16 episode. Mk.gee will make his debut as a musical guest on the show. The 50th season of SNL has featured notable impersonations, including Maya Rudolph as Vice President Kamala Harris and Dana Carvey as President Joe Biden. The show returns on Nov. 2 with host John Mulaney and musical guest Chappell Roan.
