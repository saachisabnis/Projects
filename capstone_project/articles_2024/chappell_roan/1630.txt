It's going to be a big night for Taylor Swift and Sabrina Carpenter!
According to Yahoo! Taylor Swift and Sabrina Carpenter have scored themselves quite a few Billboard music award nominations! 
They note that while Taylor has earned her 16 nods, Sabrina isn't too far behind with 9.
Yahoo! also reports that first time nominees have also come onto the scene guns blazing! Teddy Swims and Tyla earned themselves 8 nominations, Benson Boone and Shaboozey are coming in hot with with 6, and Chappell Roan has scored herself 2.
The Billboard Music Awards will air on FOX on December 12.
