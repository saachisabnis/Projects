David Gilmour has scored his first number one album in nine years (Polly Samson/Official Charts Company/PA)
David Gilmour has scored his first UK number one album in nine years with Luck And Strange.
The record is the former Pink Floyd guitarist’s third number one album after 2015’s Rattle That Lock and 2006’s On An Island.
