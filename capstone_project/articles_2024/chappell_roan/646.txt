We’re back at the Effra Social this Friday (1st November) for a Halloween themed free night of fun, throwing down coffin-rattling bangers like Thriller, Ghost Town,  Monster Mash and more  – plus the usual monster pop hits!
We’ll be shimmying in behind the decks at 10pm  and playing tunes and crazy videos until 1am – and it’s free all night, so we hope to see you on the dancefloor!
