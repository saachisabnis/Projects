ROGERS, Ark. (KY3) - Pop sensation and Willard, Mo., native Chappell Roan returned home to the Ozarks on Wednesday.
The singer played at a sold-out Walmart Amp venue in Rogers. Many more fans lined up outside the outdoor venue to hear her concert.
She played her hits, including “Hot To Go!” and “Good Luck, Babe!” She also joined the University of Arkansas cheerleaders and mascots to “Call the Hogs.”
Roan, 26, recently won the award for Best New Artist for her video “Good Luck, Babe!” Roan had canceled two previous festival appearances in the Washington, D.C., area and New York City. She wrote on Instagram, “Things have gotten overwhelming over the past few weeks, and I am really feeling it.”
