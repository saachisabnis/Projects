The Brixton Buzz team will be back at the Effra Social in Brixton tonight (Friday  20th Dec) for our Christmas Party special  – and we hope you’ll come along and join in the fun!
As ever, you can expect another big, free night of fun, with DJs throwing down huge party tunes from the likes of Taylor Swift, ABBA, Chappell Roam, Cher and Fleetwood Mac, plus disco, Britpop, 80s, 90s and 00s and some festive bangers!
