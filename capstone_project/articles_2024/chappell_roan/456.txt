Didn’t grab tickets to the 2024 Outside Lands music festival but still want to check out the scene?
Well, believe it or not, there is a way to do just that.
The festival will once again be streaming a bunch of its performances online in 2024.
The livestreams take place via Amazon Prime Video and the Amazon Music Twitch channel.
More than eight hours of music will be streamed each day during the festival, which runs Aug. 9-11 at Golden Gate Park in San Francisco and features such acts as The Killers, Sabrina Carpenter, Sturgill Simpson, Post Malone, Grace Jones, Chappell Roan and Slowdive.
The stream starts at 1:30 p.m. each day.
For more information, visit https://sfoutsidelands.com/news/tune-in-to-the-outside-lands-livestream/.
