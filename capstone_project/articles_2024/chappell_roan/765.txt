Hangout Fest is heading back to Gulf Shores, Alabama this May with a stacked lineup of performers. Led by Lana Del Rey, Zach Bryan and ODESZA, the 13th installation of the three-day festival features a wide range of talent from across the music industry including The Chainsmokers, NLE Choppa and Sexyy Red.
Elsewhere on the slate, Cage The Elephant, Dominic Fike, Renée Rapp, Jessie Murph, Doechii, Alison Wonderland, Chappell Roan and Nelly nab spots on the lineup. Check out the full list of performers in the Instagram post below.

Del Rey finds herself in a headlining spot after being scheduled to perform at the 2020 Hangout Fest, which was canceled due to COVID-19. As for ODESZA, the acclaimed DJ-duo is set to deliver a dynamic performance as one of the final shows of The Last Goodbye era.
Hangout Fest 2024 hits the beaches of Gulf Shore the weekend of May 17 – May 19, 2024. Set times have not been announced yet, but stay tuned to Hypebeast for more details on the summer festival as they surface.
Presale registration is open now at the festival’s website. Presale begins this Friday, January 12 at 11 a.m. CET.
