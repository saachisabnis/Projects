Jelly Roll scores his first No. 1 album on the Billboard 200, as Beautifully Broken debuts on top of the chart this week. Beautifully Broken is Jelly Roll’s second album to break into the Top 10 of the Billboard 200 chart, joining Whitsitt Chapel which reached No. 3 in 2023. This marks the fifth different country album to top the Billboard 200 chart in 2024, the most in a year since 2014. Elsewhere in the Billboard 200 Top 10, Rod Wave’s Last Lap debuts at No. 2, Charli XCX’s Brat jumps from No. 14 to No. 3 following a deluxe reissue, GloRilla’s Glorious premieres at No. 5, and BigXthaPlug’s Take Care debuts at No. 8. Sabrina Carpenter’s Short n’ Sweet drops two spots to No. 4, Chappell Roan’s The Rise and Fall of a Midwest Princess drops 3 slots to No. 6, Billie Eilish’s Hit Me Hard and Soft slides down two spots to No. 7, Morgan Wallen’s One Thing at a Time drops 5 slots to No. 9, and Taylor Swift’s The Tortured Poets Department slides down four spots to No. 10. (Billboard)
