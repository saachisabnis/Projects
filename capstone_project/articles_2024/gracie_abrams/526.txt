They've counted down the days and tallied up the miles to get here, and now it's time to spend, spend, spend as Taylor Swift fans line up to buy Eras Tour merchandise near the Caesars Superdome.
Hundreds of Swifties have flocked to the merch stands set up in Champions Square since they opened Wednesday.
Shopping for Taylor Swift concert merch in Champion's Square in New Orleans on Wednesday, October 23, 2024. (Photo by Chris Granger, The Times-Picayune)
Many are waiting for the coveted blue crew neck sweatshirt that is only available at the Eras Tour stops. Other items are also available online.
Still, whether they are in it for the experience of standing in line with like-minded Swifties or the desire to save on shipping, more than just ticket holders will be lining up to buy shirts, posters and more.
