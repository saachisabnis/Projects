Taylor Swift’s 2024 “Eras Tour” is slowly coming to an end. One of her last tour dates is coming right up in Indianapolis on November 1-3 at 7 p.m. Tickets are still available at a variety of price points and selling out quick, but they may be one of the cheapest ways to see her before the year is over.
Who will be opening for Taylor Swift on tour?
Singer Gracie Abrams is rumored to be opening on most of Taylor Swift’s U.S. tour dates.

What’s the setlist for the ‘Eras Tour’?
Taylor Swift’s new album “The Tortured Poets Department” was released while she was on break and the following are rumored to have made it into the new version of her 45-song setlist including songs from:
“Lover,” “Fearless,” “Red,” “Speak Now,” “Reputation,” “Folklore,” “Evermore,” “1989,″ “The Tortured Poets Department”, and “Midnights”.
Will Swift’s boyfriend Travis Kelce make an appearance on tour?
