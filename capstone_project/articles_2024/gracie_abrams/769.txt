Women dominated the 66th annual Grammys, with big wins for Taylor Swift and Miley Cyrus.
Even Barbie - the movie that spiced up the summer and set the box office alight - received a win, thanks to Billie Eilish's song from the soundtrack.
The celebs showed off their looks ahead of the ceremony, with Swift, Kylie Minogue, Eilish, Dua Lipa, Ed Sheeran and Paris Hilton among the A-list guests.
