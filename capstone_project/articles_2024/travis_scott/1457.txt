Local police officers make three arrests
AUBURN — Police made these arrests Aug. 11-12 according to DeKalb County Jail records.
Blake Bigger, 35, of the 7000 block of Elmbrook Drive, Fort Wayne, was arrested at 11:48 a.m. Aug. 11 by the DeKalb County Sheriff’s Office on a warrant alleging dealing methamphetamine, a Level 2 felony; unlawful possession of a firearm by a felon, a Level 4 felony; and possession of a narcotic drug and theft of a firearm, both Level 5 felonies.
Travis Scott, 34. of the 6200 block of C.R. 31, Auburn, was arrested at 6:01 p.m. Aug. 12 by the DeKalb County Sheriff’s Office on a warrant alleging domestic battery, a Level 6 felony. Scott posted $50 cash bond to be released at 7:57 p.m. Aug. 12.
Dexter Crossley, 35, of the 5500 block of McClean Street, Fort Wayne, was arrested at 10:50 p.m. Aug. 12 by Auburn Police on charges of domestic battery, a Level 6 felony; and disorderly conduct, a Class B misdemeanor. Crossley was being held without bond until seen in court.
