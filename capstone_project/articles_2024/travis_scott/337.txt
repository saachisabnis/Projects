TULARE, Calif. (KSEE/KGPE) – Two people have been detained after officers found a person dead in suspicious circumstances in Tulare, according to the city’s police department.
Police say just after midnight on Wednesday, officers were dispatched to an address in the 700 block of South R. Street in Tulare. Officers arrived and found two people in the driveway of a home. Police say they appeared nervous and officers saw what they thought was blood on the ground and the suspects’ clothing.
Police say officers eventually made entry to the home and inside found a dead person – identified as 48-year-old Travis Womack of Tulare.
The two subjects were immediately detained for further investigation. Detectives established the dead person was a victim of a homicide and that the two detained subjects were involved.
Police say the two suspects were transported to the Tulare Police Department where they were booked, processed and interviewed. The suspects have been identified as 48-year-old Sakuma Lockhard from Tulare and 26-year-old Steven Elizondo of Delano. The motive for the homicide is still under investigation.
This is an ongoing investigation and anyone with any information regarding this incident is urged to contact Detective Dan Scott at 559-685-2300 ext. 2153 or Sgt. Eddie Hinojosa at 559-684-4390.
