Travis Scott has a special treat for his day one fans as he'll be re-releasing his breakout mixtape, Days Before Rodeo, which will be accompanied by a special merchandise collection.
On Sunday, Scott posted a trailer across his social platforms announcing the re-release of his groundbreaking mixtape in honor of the project's tenth anniversary. The video contained archival footage of La Flame recording the mixtape alongside Metro Boomin, Future, Young Thug, Southside, and more, while also performing on the Days Before Rodeo Tour.
"DAYS BEFORE RODEO 10yr ANNIVERSARY BEING CELEBRATED BY FINALLLY RE-RELEASING EVERYWHERE AUGUST 23RD AND MAYBE COUPLE DBR SONGS FROM THAT ERA FROM THE VAULT Travisscott.com more info and shizzzzshsbsbsbsb IM FCKING JUMPING THRU WALLS AHHHH," Scott captioned his Instagram post.
The mixtape's re-release is labeled as a digital deluxe edition and is scheduled to drop on all streaming platforms for the first time on August 23. The deluxe edition will include unreleased bonus tracks from the Days Before Rodeo era and exclusive merch and vinyl bundles, all available on travisscott.com.
Days Before Rodeo was initially released in 2014 and served as La Flame's second official mixtape after his debut a year before with Owl Pharoah. It had a stacked list of guest features, including Young Thug, Big Sean, Migos, and more. On the production side, Metro Boomin, Lex Luger, FKi, Wondagurl, and Scott helped usher in a new tone for hip-hop with the mixtape with its gritty, bombastic sound.
Standout tracks include "Don't Play" featuring Big Sean, "Mamacita" featuring Rich Homie Quan and Young Thug, and "Quintana Pt. 2" featuring T.I.
