ESSEX, Mo. (KFVS) - Three people were arrested in Stoddard County after being found with stolen scrap metal.
On January 24, the Stoddard County Sheriff’s Office received a call in reference to stealing on State Highway 153 near Essex, Missouri. Upon arriving at the location, Deputy Travis Maddox located a white pickup truck and trailer loaded with scrap metal.
Deputy Maddox located two male subjects and one female subject. They were later identified as 45-year-old Jessie Hernandez of Sikeston, 37-year-old Donya L. Smith of St. Louis, and 45-year-old Angela Smith of Sikeston.
During the investigation, it was determined the scrap metal items on the truck and trailer had been stolen from the property. All three subjects were arrested and transported to the Stoddard County Jail. They are currently being housed on the following charges:
Hernandez and Angela Smith’s bonds were set at $5,000 cash or surety. Donya Smith bond was set at $10,000 cash or surety. All three are currently housed in the Stoddard County Jail.
