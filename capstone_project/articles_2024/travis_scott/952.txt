A fatal shooting occurred in Santa Monica on Thursday, resulting in the death of 46-year-old Edward Williams III. The Santa Monica Police Department received reports of shots fired and found the victim in his car, later pronounced dead at the hospital. Authorities identified the alleged shooter as 27-year-old homeless woman Kayla Mackey. The motive for the shooting is unknown, and witnesses reported that the victim, known for his work in the music industry, was wearing headphones at the time. Edward Williams III leaves behind two young boys and had a recording studio in Hollywood, working with artists such as Travis Scott and Lil Cobaine.
