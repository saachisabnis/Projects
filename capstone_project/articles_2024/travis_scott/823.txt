Viewers caught tons of unforgettable moments during the star-studded 66th Annual Grammy Awards on Sunday evening (February 4), hosted once again by comedian and actor Trevor Noah at Crypto.com Arena in Los Angeles, California.
It comes to no surprise that the Grammys featured an all-star lineup of performers, presenters and award winners taking the stage that evening, including Billie Eilish, Billy Joel, Burna Boy, Dua Lipa, Joni Mitchell, Luke Combs — who is reportedly performing his CMA Award-winning cover of “Fast Car” with original singer-songwriter Tracy Chapman — Olivia Rodrigo, SZA, Travis Scott, U2. Christina Aguilera, Samara Joy, Lenny Kravitz, Maluma, Lionel Richie, Mark Ronson, Meryl Streep, Taylor Tomlinson and Oprah Winfrey. The show honored some of the best accomplishments in music across many coveted genre-specific and all-genre categories. Some artists added to their growing collection of Grammy Award victories while others marked their first-time wins on Sunday night. See the list of 2024 Grammy Award winners throughout the evening here.
There were many moments, however, that took place at the Los Angeles venue that happened backstage, that viewers didn’t see when they watched the awards show on TV.
