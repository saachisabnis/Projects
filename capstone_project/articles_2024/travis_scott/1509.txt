Future has released his first solo project of the year, “Mixtape Pluto,” featuring 17 tracks including "Too Fast" and "Lil Demon." 
The album has received support from peers like Travis Scott, who teased a track called "South of France." This is Future's third project of 2024, following two collaborative albums with Metro Boomin.
 “Mixtape Pluto” is his first solo project since 2022's Grammy-nominated album, I Never Liked You.
