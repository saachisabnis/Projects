A recent article in Forbes magazine that recognizes Journey's "Don't Stop Believin'" as "The Biggest Song of All Time."
Steve Perry, who co-wrote the 1981 song with Schon and Jonathan Cain, says, "When this 'Don’t Stop Believin’' - ”The Biggest Song of ALL Time' article came out I was so emotionally stunned.
"Don't Stop Believin'" has been certified 18x platinum, placing it in a tie with “Sunflower (Spider-man: Into the Spiderverse)” by Post Malone and Swae Lee as the highest certified single ever in the U.S.
