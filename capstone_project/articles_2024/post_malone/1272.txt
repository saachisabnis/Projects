ROCKFORD, Ill. (WIFR) - Shane Gillis announced Tuesday morning via Instagram he will be playing the BMO Center on June 14.
Artist presale tickets went on sale at 10 a.m. Wednesday. Tickets for the general public were supposed to go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday, but sold out in 58 minutes.
If you don’t already know the name, Shane Gillis has been dominating the comedy world on many fronts.
Gillis and fellow comedian Matt McCusker have a podcast called “Matt and Shane’s Secret Podcast,” which is the top podcast on Patreon with over 82,000 paid members.
Alongside friend and writer John McKeever, Gillis writes and stars in skits called “Gilly and Keeves.”
His debut stand-up comedy special “Shane Gillis Live In Austin” has accrued more than 24 million views on YouTube. The success of that special grabbed the attention of streaming mogul Netflix, which released his second special titled “Beautiful Dogs.”
His success has grabbed the attention of comedy giants like Louis C.K., Dave Chappelle, Tom Segura and more. Other famous names outside of comedy to take a liking to Gillis are Jack Harlow, Post Malone, Gabe Davis and many more.
