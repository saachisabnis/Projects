Taylor Swift is a marketing genius.  Her latest album ‘The Tortured Poets Department, isn’t even out yet and she announced an exclusive second version with a bonus track. 
Swift announced that The Bolter, featuring an exclusive bonus track and new cover will be the second version of her new album. The Bolter is available for preorder in various formats, the album drops on April 19. 
Swift shared the news during her Eras Tour show in Melbourne, where she performed surprise songs and expressed the importance of songwriting in her life. The Tortured Poets Department, announced at the Grammys, includes collaborations with Florence + The Machine and Post Malone.
Swift also performed acoustic guitar rendition of “Red” and played “You’re Losing Me” on piano.  She also played, “You’re Losing Me” which is one of Swift’s favorite songs from Tortured Poets.  Swift shared that songwriting on Tortured Poets helped her through life.
