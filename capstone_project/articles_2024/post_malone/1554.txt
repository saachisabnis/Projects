This year’s MTV VMAs took place on the 23rd anniversary of the 9/11 terror attacks – a scheduling shift caused by Tuesday’s debate between Kamala Harris and Donald Trump.
To mark the solemn anniversary, the red carpet at New York’s UBS Arena was switched to jet black and the stars that graced it opted for a respectfully gothic style.
