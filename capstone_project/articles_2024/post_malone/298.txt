ORANGE BEACH, Ala. -- Post Malone will be in Orange Beach in October.
The rapper and now country singer announced he'll perform at The Wharf Amphitheater on Oct. 17. It will be his second-to-last show of his F-1 Trillion Tour.
Tickets will be available starting with a Citi presale beginning Wednesday at 9 a.m. CT. Additional presales will run throughout the week ahead of the general on-sale beginning July 1 at 9 a.m. CT. on livenation.com
The tour will feature a collection of country songs as the singer recently transitioned into the new genre with his No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 "I Had Some Help" featuring country star Morgan Wallen.
The singer's upcoming tour will stop in other major cities including Atlanta, Charlotte and New York.
