SYRACUSE, N.Y. (WSYR-TV) — American rapper turned country artist Post Malone stops at Upstate New York restaurant chain Dinosaur BBQ in Syracuse before his headliner show at the Empower Federal Credit Union Ampiltheather at Lakeview.
The restaurant took to its social media accounts on Facebook and Instagram to welcome Malone back to his hometown city.
Courtesy of Dinosaur BBQ via Facebook.
The Post Malone concert is set to begin tonight, September 14 at 8 p.m. Tickets for the concert are sold out but resale lawn tickets are starting $147.
