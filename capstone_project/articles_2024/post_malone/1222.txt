Bonnaroo has unveiled the full slate of headliners and performers for its 2024 festival. Fred Again.., Post Malone, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Pretty Lights and FISHER lead the charge, backed by a genre-bending slate of accompanying performers including Megan Thee Stallion, Teezo Touchdown, Faye Webster, T-Pain, Thundercat and Yves Tumor.
Bonnaroo is set to take over Manchester, Tennessee’s Bonnaroo Farm beginning Thursday, June 13 – Sunday, June 16, hosting over 150 performers and vendors.
Other artists set to hit the Bonnaroo stage over the course of the weekend include Maggie Rogers, Dominic Fike, Key Glock, Cage the Elephant, Jon Batiste, Ethel Cain, Joey Bada$$ and Róisín Murphy. Check out the full lineup below.

The festival also hosts an annual “SuperJam,” with this year’s dubbed “One More With Feeling(s) — The Dashboard Confessional Emo Superjam.”
“Outeroo & late night sets to be announced,” the festival added in an Instagram caption. Pretty Lights is also set to deliver a “Sunrise Set” as well.
Pre-sale begins this Thursday, January 11 at 10 a.m. CET, with general onsale kicking off at a later date, “if tickets and accommodations are still available.” Sign up for a presale code – and view the full festival information – at Bonnaroo’s website.
