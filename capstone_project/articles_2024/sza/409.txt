SZA – who leads this year’s Grammy nominations with a total of nine – will also be performing live at the ceremony this Sunday. She joins a star-studded lineup that also includes Travis Scott, Dua Lipa, Burna Boy, Billie Eilish, Luke Combs, Travis Scott, Olivia Rodrigo, Billy Joel, and Joni Mitchell, who will perform at the ceremony for the first time. U2 will also perform live from their Sphere residency in Las Vegas. The Grammy Awards will air this Sunday, February 4th on CBS and Paramount+. SZA will also be featured on a new episode of the concert series Apple Music Live on Wednesday from her SOS tour stop in New York City. The full concert debuts tomorrow at 10 p.m. ET on Apple Music and Apple TV+.
