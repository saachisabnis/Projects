The biggest stars in music are being honored during the Grammy Awards on Sunday, February 4. Before the awards show kicks off, several of the musicians got to show off their unique styles while walking the red carpet.
Nominees including Taylor Swift, SZA, Miley Cyrus, Billie Eilish and more walked the red carpet before the awards were presented.
Keep scrolling to see what the nominees and presenters wore at the 2024 Grammys!
