Ebony’s annual Power 100 list has been revealed, celebrating Black talent across various industries, including veterans like Snoop Dogg, Maya Rudolph, Keke Palmer, Gayle King, SZA and Kevin Hart, alongside newcomers like basketball star Angel Reese. The awards gala, marking the magazine’s 79th anniversary, will be held on November 17th in Los Angeles. What is now the Power 100 list began in 1971 as Ebony’s “Most Influential Black Americans List,” celebrating the best in Black expression and cultural impact. “This iconic list is not just a celebration; it’s a vital recognition of those who are shaping culture and driving real change in our communities,” said Ebony CEO Eden Bridgeman.
