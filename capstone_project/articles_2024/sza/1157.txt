SZA opens up about the making of her iconic debut studio album Ctrl. In an exclusive interview with iHeartRadio, the TDE singer explaina how her inner circle of friends influenced her first body of work and what she learned about herself throughout the recording process. She reveals that making the project inspired her to become her own therapist as she vented about her everything going on in her life. Later on, she revisits Pharrell Williams contribution to the intro "Supermodel" and gushes about his whispered vocals. "His whisper is magnificent," she says.
