21 Savage remained at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 albums chart, as American Dream spends its second week in the top spot. Morgan Wallen’s One Thing At A Time rose back up to No. 2, and Drake’s For All the Dogs climbed back to No. 3. Green Day’s Saviors debuted at No. 4, marking the 12th top 10 album of their career. The first single from the record, “The American Dream Is Killing Me,” also landed at No. 2 on the Alternative Airplay chart. Noah Kahan’s Stick Season rounded out the top 5, knocking Taylor Swift’s 1989 (Taylor’s Version) out of the top 5 (to No. 6) this week. SZA’s SOS lands at No. 7, Nicki Minaj’s Pink Friday 2 drops one spot to No. 8, Zach Bryan’s self-titled album to No. 9 and Swift’s Lover remains steady at No. 10.
