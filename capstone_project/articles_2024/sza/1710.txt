Recently, Drake released the video for the song “Rich Baby Daddy” featuring SZA and Sexyy Red. Drake shot the video and features the rapper using a camcorder to capture moments between the three artists. 
The video has gone viral since its release, and the rapper took to social media to give thanks to the two ladies. “Thanks to the girls for trusting me when they showed up and their was no trailers and no lights it was just me holding a camcorder saying I promise this will work 😂,” said Drake. From there, he thanked his collaborators on the video side and also showed love to both artists via his photos. Take a look above.
