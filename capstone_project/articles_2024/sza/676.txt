Taylor Swift’s 1989 (Taylor’s Version) remained at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 albums chart for a sixth non-consecutive week. (She actually has four albums in the top 10, with Midnights, Lover and Folklore at Nos. 5, 6 and 10, respectively.) Morgan Wallen’s former No. 1 blockbuster, One Thing At A Time, landed at No. 2, while Drake’s For All the Dogs rose back up to No. 3. Nicki Minaj’s new release Pink Friday 2 was No. 4, while Swift’s Midnights and Lover took No. 5 & 6 and SZA’s hit SOS landed at No. 7. Noah Kahan’s Stick Season and Zach Bryan’s self-titled were No. 8 and 9.
