Jack Harlow's “Lovin On Me” is back at No. 1 (from No. 2) for a fifth non-consecutive week atop the Billboard Hot 100. Teddy Swims' “Lose Control” rises to a new high at No. 2, while Benson Boone’s “Beautiful Things” hits No. 3 just a week after becoming his first top 10 hit. Taylor Swift’s “Cruel Summer” landed at No. 4 this week, while SZA’s “Snooze” jumped from No. 10 all the way back up to No. 5.
Elsewhere, Tracy Chapman's “Fast Car” re-entered the Hot 100 for the first time in 35 years (at No. 42) after she performed it with Luke Combs at the Grammys last weekend.
