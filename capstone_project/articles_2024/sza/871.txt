From Miley Cyrus to Taylor Swift, the 66th annual Grammy Awards are bringing music’s hottest musicians under one roof!
The prestigious event, held at Los Angeles’ Crypto.com arena on Sunday, February 4, brings out the industries’ most influential and fans can expect out-of-this-world performances from SZA, Dua Lipa, Burna Boy and Travis Scott.
While viewers watch the award show for the music, they stay for the red carpet fashion!
Keep scrolling to see the best and worst celebrity looks at the 2024 Grammys!
