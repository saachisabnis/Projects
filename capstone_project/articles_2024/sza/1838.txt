Taylor Swift's 1989 (Taylor's Version) is No. 1 on the U.S. album chart for a third consecutive week.

               Coming in at No. 2 on the Billboard 200 chart dated Saturday is Morgan Wallen's One Thing at a Time, followed by Drake's For All the Dogs at No. 3, Nicki Minaj's Pink Friday 2 at No. 4 and Swift's Midnights at No. 5.
Rounding out the top tier are Swift's Lover at No. 6, SZA's SOS at No. 7, Noah Kahan's Stick Season at No. 8, Zach Bryan's self-titled album at No. 9 and Swift's Folklore at No. 10.
