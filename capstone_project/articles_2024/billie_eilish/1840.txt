It was just announced that Ryan Gosling will be performing his song from the "Barbie" film, "I'm Just Ken," live at the Oscars on March 10th.
Mark Ronson wrote "I'm Just Ken" with Andrew Wyatt and it has been nominated for best original song at the Oscars along with another song from the film - "What Was I Made For?" by Billie Eilish. The 96th annual Oscars will air on ABC on March 10th at 4pm PT. For more, click here.
