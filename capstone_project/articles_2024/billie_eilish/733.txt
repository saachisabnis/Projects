Over the years, Billie Eilish has taken initiative to advocate for sustainability. In 2019, she teamed up with Global Citizen to offer free concert tickets to environmental conscious fans. In 2022, the pop star launched a climate action event called Overheated. She opened up about the issue in a new Billboard interview, in which she called out artists who are “wasteful” by releasing dozens of vinyl variants.
