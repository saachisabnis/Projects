Shaboozey’s “A Bar Song (Tipsy)” returned to the top of the Billboard Hot 100 chart, spending its 16th week at No. 1. Shaboozey’s crossover hit ties the record for the longest reign at No. 1 of the 2020s, matching Morgan Wallen’s 16-week stint at No. 1 with “Last Night” in 2023. Tyler, the Creator, whose new record, Chromakopia, debuted at No. 1 on the album chart, notches his first two Billboard Top 10 singles, with “St. Chroma” debuting at No. 7 and “Noid” jumping 33 spots to No. 10. Elsewhere in the Hot 100’s Top 10, Lady Gaga and Bruno Mars’ “Die With a Smile” jumps up two spots to No. 2, Billie Eilish’s “Birds of a Feather” stays at No. 3, Sabrina Carpenter’s “Espresso” moves up a spot to No. 4 while her single “Taste” remains at No. 9, Post Malone’s “I Had Some Help,” featuring Morgan Wallen, moves up a spot to No. 5, Teddy Swims’ “Lose Control,” slides up a slot to No. 6, and Wallen’s “Love Somebody” drops to No. 8 after debuting at No. 1 last week. (Billboard)
