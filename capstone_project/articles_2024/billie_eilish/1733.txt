Billie Eilish stopped by for a very London lunch to chat all things 'Hit Me Hard And Soft' in an exclusive interview with Capital's Jordan North.
To celebrate the release of her third studio album 'Hit Me Hard And Soft', Billie Eilish joined Capital Breakfast presenter Jordan North to unpack her latest project over a very London lunch.
On the reaction to the album Billie said: "It's been like the most shocking reaction of my life, it's been so good I can't even believe my eyes every time.
"It's been better than I ever could have dreamed off in a million years."
Jordan asked Billie if this is her most personal album to date, to which she said: "For sure."Watch their full interview on Global Player.
