Pop music star Taylor Swift's Tortured Poets Department is No. 1 on the U.S. album chart for a ninth week.

               Coming in at No. 2 on the Billboard 200 chart dated Saturday is Billie Eilish's Hit Me Hard and Soft, followed by Don Toliver's Hardstone Psycho at No. 3, Morgan Wallen's One Thing at a Time at No. 4 and $uicideboy$'s New World Depression at No. 5.
Rounding out the top tier are Luke Combs' Fathers & Sons at No. 6, Nayeon's NA: The 2nd Mini Album at No. 7, Chappell Roan's The Rise and Fall of a Midwest Princess at No. 8, Charli XCX's Brat at No. 9 and Morgan Wallen's Dangerous: The Double Album at No. 10.
