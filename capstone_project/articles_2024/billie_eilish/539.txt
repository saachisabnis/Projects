The alternative rock band, Weezer, is stopping at the TD Garden in Boston, MA on Tuesday, September 10 as part of their ”Voyage to the Blue Planet” tour celebrating the 30th anniversary of their debut album, the “Blue Album.” 
Fans can still shop for tickets to the show on Tuesday night for under $50 by using reliable third-party ticket sellers like StubHub, VividSeats and SeatGeek. 
As of Monday, September 9, the cheapest tickets to the show in Boston, MA are $31 on StubHub, $37 on VividSeats and $35 on SeatGeek.
The Band will play the album in full on their tour and The Flaming Lips and Dinosaur Jr. will be joining them as supporting acts. 
Weezer kicked off the tour in Saint Paul, MN on September 4 and will make stops in New York, Philadelphia, Orlando, Nashville and more.
