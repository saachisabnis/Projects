Chase And Status and Stormzy pose with their official number one single awards from Official Charts Company for Backbone (Official Charts Company/PA)
Stormzy and Chase And Status have sailed to number one in the UK singles chart with their collaborative offering Backbone.
The track merges their worlds of grime and drum and bass and has become the electronic duo’s first number one single and Stormzy’s fourth.
