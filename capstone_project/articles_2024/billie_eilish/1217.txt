Billie Eilish fans will be able to get to her shows while also helping the planet!
According to Google, the singer is teaming up with the Maps App to help fans get to her concert, and plant based eateries nearby ecologically!
They note that in a statement Billie said, "I am so excited to see you all at my shows over the coming months, and for us to work together in reducing our collective footprint when it comes to transportation and what we eat... Every action matters, no matter how big or small, and together we can truly begin to heal our beautiful planet. Thanks to Google Maps, everyone will have easy access to resources that will help you make great sustainable choices when you come to my shows. Thank you for caring. See you soon!"
The measures taken in the initiatives include a list of Billie's favorite plant-based restaurants, bus routes, and ecologically friendly driving routes.
