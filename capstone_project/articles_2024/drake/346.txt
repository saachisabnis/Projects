CHERRYVILLE, N.C. (WBTV) - A man wanted for attempted first-degree murder in South Carolina was arrested in Gaston County on Wednesday, police said.
The Gaston County Police Department said 27-year-old Benjamin Drake Green was arrested in the area of Doc Wehunt Road in Cherryville.
Green had been considered armed and dangerous and eluded law enforcement for several months.
Authorities were able to arrest him after receiving a tip from a concerned citizen.
Green is being held in the Gaston County Jail until he is extradited back to South Carolina.
Jail records show that he was also charged with possession of methamphetamine upon his arrest.
