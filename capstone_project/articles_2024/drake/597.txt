• The Marin Art and Garden Center’s summer concert series continues with performances by SonoTriette at 5:30 p.m. Thursday and the Cottontails at 5:30 p.m. Aug. 29 at 30 Sir Francis Drake Blvd. in Ross. Admission is $20 in advance and $25 at the gate; youth age 17 and younger are free. For more information and to buy tickets, go to maringarden.org/summerconcerts.
