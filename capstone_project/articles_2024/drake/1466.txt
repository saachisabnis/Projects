			Trending:		
• Annie Baker’s “The Antipodes,” performed by the Actors’ Reading Collective in its West Coast premiere, kicks off with a preview performance at 7:30 p.m. Nov. 8 at Marin Shakespeare Company’s indoor theater at 514 Fourth St. in San Rafael. The show opens at 7:30 p.m. Nov. 9 and runs through Dec. 1. Admission is $20 to $100. For more information, to buy tickets and for a complete list of dates and times, visit marinshakespeare.org.
• Ross Valley Players’ production of Noël Coward’s “Blithe Spirit” kicks off with a preview performance at 7:30 p.m. Nov. 14 at the Barn Theater at 30 Sir Francis Drake Blvd. in Ross. The show opens at 7:30 p.m. Nov. 15 and continues through Dec. 15. Admission is $20 for the preview show and $20 to $35 for regular performances. For more information, to buy tickets and for a complete list of dates and times, visit rossvalleyplayers.com.
• Marin musical theater and cabaret singer Craig Jessup performs his one-man show “Where I Want To Be” at 8 p.m. Nov. 16 at the Throckmorton Theatre at 142 Throckmorton Ave. in Mill Valley. Admission is $48 to $58. For more information and to buy tickets, go to throckmortontheatre.org.
• Louise Roche’s musical “Twas A Girls Night Before Christmas” comes to Marin at 3 p.m. Dec. 1 at the Marin Center Showcase Theater at 20 Avenue of the Flags in San Rafael. Admission is $51 to $73. For more information and to buy tickets, visit tickets.marincenter.org.
