OSHTEMO TOWNSHIP, Mich. (WOOD) — A 23-year-old man was killed in an apartment fire in Oshtemo Township Saturday morning.
Just before 8 a.m., deputies with the Kalamazoo County Sheriff’s Office were sent to assist fire crews at an apartment on S Drake Road near Green Meadow Road for a fire.
The sheriff’s office said that a 23-year-old Kalamazoo man was found in the apartment where the fire was. First responders attempted life-saving measures but he died at the scene. His name has not been released.
The fire appears to have been accidental, the sheriff’s office said.
Anyone with information is asked to contact the Kalamazoo County Sheriff’s Office at 269.383.8748 or Silent Observer at 269.343.2100.
The fire remains under investigation.
