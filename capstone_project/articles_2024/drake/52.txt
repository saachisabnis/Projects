DES MOINES, Iowa (KCRG) - The Drake women’s basketball earned the season sweep over Northern Iowa after the Panthers fell 79-77 in a close battle at the Knapp Center. 
Maya McDermott led the Panther offense with 24 points and four assists. This marked McDermott’s seventh 20+-point performance of the season. Four other Panthers were in double-figures in points.
UNI returns to Cedar Falls for their final home series of the regular season on Thursday, Feb. 29. against Valparaiso.
