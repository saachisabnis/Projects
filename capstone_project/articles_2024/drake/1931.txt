LEBANON, N.H. (WCAX) - Police in New Hampshire are asking the public for help finding a missing Dartmouth graduate student.
Lebanon police say Kexin Cai, 26, was last seen at about 3 p.m. on May 15 in the area of Drake Lane in West Lebanon.
They say Cai left her place on an e-bike, headed in an unknown direction. The bike is silver and white with “Fatboy” tires, a thick frame and heavy-duty black metal racks on the front and back.
Police say Cai is 5′ 3″, 105 pounds, with black eyes and black and blue hair.
Anyone with information on Cai is asked to contact the Lebanon police at 603-448-1212.
