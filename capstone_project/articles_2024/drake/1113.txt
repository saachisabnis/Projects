Kendrick Lamar is having an incredibly successful year. The rapper from Compton has won his well-known feud with Drake and set records with his popular diss track "Not Like Us." Now, he may be on the verge of even more success. He has received seven nominations for the 2025 Grammys, with five of them for "Not Like Us." This song is up for awards like Song of the Year, Record of the Year, and Best Music Video. Additionally, the track "Like That" by Future and Metro Boomin, featuring Kendrick's intense verse that sparked the feud, is nominated for two awards. Social media is buzzing with reactions, and many fans believe Kendrick deserves these nominations, expecting him to win at least a few. Fans are also referencing Drake's song "Family Matters," where he seemed to predict this moment, saying, "Kendrick just opened his mouth, someone go hand him a Grammy right now." This line has become a source of humor for Kendrick's supporters. Kendrick has not yet commented on his nominations. Besides potential Grammy wins, Kendrick has more exciting news ahead. In February, he will headline the Super Bowl halftime show in New Orleans.
