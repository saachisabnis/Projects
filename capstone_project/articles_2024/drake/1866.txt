On Thursday, members of the Kalamazoo Valley Enforcement Team, or KVET, executed a search warrant on South Drake Road in the Township of Oshtemo following an investigation.
KVET investigators seized approximately 11 ounces of methamphetamine and a quantity of ecstasy pills.
U.S. currency believed to be profits of drug sales and drug manufacturing and selling equipment were also seized.
A 38-year-old male suspect, who is currently on parole, was arrested and lodged at the Kalamazoo County Jail for multiple felony charges.
