KANSAS CITY, MO. (WIBW) - After its overtime loss to Drake at the T-Mobile Center Tuesday night, Coleman Hawkins opened  up on some of the backlash he and his team receives.
“Me personally, I feel like it’s us versus everybody. I can’t go on my phone without people wishing the worst for me and not just for me, but for our team and it devastates me. I went on my phone and saw someone say, ‘I hope you break your leg’. That’s the kind of stuff that our team has been dealing with all year,” Hawkins said.
The ‘Cats now head to Wichita State on Saturday for its last non-conference game with tip-off at six p.m. on ESPN+.
