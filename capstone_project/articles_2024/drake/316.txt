It's been a rough week for Drake!
According to CNN, just one day after a security guard was shot outside of Drake's Toronto home, it was the subject an attempted break in!
They note that a rep for Toronto police said the attempt happened around 2pm local time. Police responded "after a person attempted to gain access to the property."
The suspect was taken into custody and taken to receive medical care. CNN notes that under Toronto's Mental Health Act, officers who feel someone is suffering from a mental disorder can take them in for assistance.
