Colton Underwood, widely known from Bachelor Nation, has announced that he and his husband, Jordan C. Brown, are expecting their first child together. The exciting news was shared by Underwood through an Instagram post on May 21, where he revealed, “Our little boy is coming this fall.”
However, the journey to parenthood was not without its challenges. The 32-year-old former reality star opened up about the complexities of the fertility process, including his struggle with a low sperm count. Underwood confessed that the diagnosis came as a surprise and that he was initially uncertain about how to address his reproductive health to conceive successfully.
“I just figured I’d be good,” Underwood revealed in an interview with Men’s Health regarding his fertility journey. “I figured Jordan would be good. We’re healthy.”
