CHICAGO — A man is wanted after an attempted kidnapping of a 13-year-old girl Friday on the Northwest Side.
At around 4:50 p.m., a 13-year-old girl was walking in the area of North Drake and West Rosemont when she was approached by a man.
He was initially parked in a dark color SUV, possibly a Land or Range Rover, in the 3500 block of West Rosemont.
He attempted to engage the girl in conversation while exiting the SUV. The girl ran away and observed the man following her.
She ran to the 6200 block of North Central Park and lost sight of the man.

The man was between 28-38, 5’6″-5’10” with a round face and dark complexion. CPD said he was wearing a Fez head dress with an all-black thawab or thobe.
Anyone with information can call police at 312-746-6554.
