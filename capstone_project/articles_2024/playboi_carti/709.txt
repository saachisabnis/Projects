Tyler, The Creator has announced a world tour in support of his forthcoming album, “Chromakopia.” The tour will make a three-night stop at the Crypto.com Arena on Feb. 14, 17, and 20, 2025.
With more than 60 arena shows across the U.S., Canada, the U.K., Europe, New Zealand and Australia, the trek will kick off on Feb. 4 in St. Paul, Minnesota at Xcel Energy Center. Tyler is also bringing a few friends along for the ride, with Lil Yachty and Paris Texas joining as support for the duration of the tour.
Tickets for the general public will go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday, Nov. 1 at Ticketmaster.com. Presale options will also be available for American Express card members starting on Oct. 29.
The tour supports Tyler’s upcoming album, scheduled for release on Oct. 28. This week, he unveiled the lead single, “Noid,” accompanied by a music video starring Ayo Edebiri, known for her role in the Emmy-winning Hulu series “The Bear.”
Tyler is also set to host his annual festival Camp Flog Gnaw at Dodger Stadium on Nov. 16-17. Tickets for the 10th-anniversary festival sold out within minutes of its announcement, although fans can still sign up for the waitlist at campfloggnaw.com.
This year, the lineup boasts Action Bronson, André 3000, The Alchemist & Friends, Amelia Moore, Blood Orange, Concrete Boys, Daniel Caesar, Denzel Curry, Doechii, Domo Genesis, Earl Sweatshirt, Erykah Badu, Faye Webster, FM Mood: A tribute to MF Doom, Hana Vu, Jean Dawson, Raye, Sampha, Tommy Richman, Jordan Ward, Kaytranada, Kenny Mason, Laila! Latin Mafia, Left Brain, Lithe, Ma$e, The Mainliners, The Marías, Omar Apollo, Playboi Carti, Sexxy Red, among others.
