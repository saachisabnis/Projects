Rich The Kid hit back at Elliott Wilson after the veteran Hip Hop journalist shaded the rapper.
Appearing to reference Rich’s appearance on Kanye West and Ty Dolla $ign’s joint album Vultures 1, Wilson wrote on X: “Rich The Kid is the luckiest n-gga on the planet.”

Rich appears on the song “Carnival” along with Playboi Carti, with the track widely seen as a standout on the divisive project. The song is also currently No. 1 on Spotify’s Global Chart.
Rich fired back at Wilson on X, writing: “How the fuck I’m lucky ? N-ggas made that song from scratch.. hating is not cool bro.”
Despite not receiving much acclaim during his career, Rich The Kid has collaborated with other Hip Hop heavyweights like Kendrick Lamar, Lil Wayne, Nicki Minaj and Future, while his debut album The World Is Yours went platinum.
His fourth album Life’s a Gamble is expected to be released in the coming months and contain more A-list guests such as Kanye West, Offset and Travis Barker.
