The Weeknd has debuted his track, “São Paulo”, featuring Brazilian superstar Anitta.
Last month, The Weeknd delivered a sold-out performance in São Paulo, which featured surprise appearances from Playboi Carti Anitta, where they debuted the track. Proceeds from the event’s merchandise benefited the Brazilian Soul Fund of BrazilFoundation, supporting communities facing natural and economic challenges in Brazil.
“São Paulo” is out now.
