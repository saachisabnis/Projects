  This past November, the highly anticipated 10-year anniversary of Camp Flog Gnaw Carnival unfolded in spectacular fashion at Dodger Stadium, and if you weren’t there, you definitely missed out on one of the most unforgettable music festivals of the year. The two-day celebration curated by Tyler, the Creator, offered an incredible blend of music, art, food, and immersive experiences that left attendees buzzing with excitement.
Unforgettable Performances
  Headliners Daniel Caesar, Playboi Carti, and Tyler, the Creator delivered jaw-dropping performances that captivated the crowd. Tyler treated fans to a performance of his new music, showcasing his continued evolution as an artist while blending it with fan-favorites. Playboi Carti also shocked the audience with fresh, unreleased tracks, bringing an electrifying energy that kept everyone hyped until the very last beat.
  Earlier in the day, Doechii lit up the stage with her fierce presence and innovative artistry. Her set had fans raving, and it’s clear she’s cementing herself as one of music’s rising stars. Other notable acts included Denzel Curry, Erykah Badu, The Alchemist & Friends, and Kaytranada, adding to the festival’s unmatched lineup.
A Feast for the Senses
Camp Flog Gnaw wasn’t just about the music—it was a complete sensory experience. The carnival rides and games brought back nostalgic fairground vibes, adding a playful element to the weekend. Foodies rejoiced at the diverse selection of gourmet food vendors offering everything from classic carnival treats to innovative culinary creations.
The Museum of Odd Future
One of the standout installations was the Museum of Odd Future, an homage to Tyler’s creative collective, Odd Future. Fans wandered through interactive exhibits and rare memorabilia that chronicled the group’s groundbreaking journey in music and culture.
Merch Mania
Merch collectors were in heaven. Exclusive booths featured Golf Wang drops, Tyler’s eye-catching Chromakopia merch, and Playboi Carti’s Opium-labeled apparel—each serving as a coveted reminder of the weekend. The unique designs and limited-edition pieces made these items instant grails for fans.
Why You Can’t Miss Next Year
The 10-year anniversary of Camp Flog Gnaw was a resounding success, blending top-tier performances, immersive art, and an atmosphere of pure joy. It left attendees with memories to cherish—and a hefty dose of bragging rights. If you missed out this year, the FOMO is real. The festival’s ability to innovate while honoring its roots makes it a can’t-miss event.
Mark your calendars for next year. Camp Flog Gnaw is only getting bigger and better, and you’ll want to be there to witness the magic firsthand. This is more than a festival; it’s a cultural moment.
