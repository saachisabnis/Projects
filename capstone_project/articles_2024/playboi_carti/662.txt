Yung Joc addressed how artist Playboi Carti, known for his unconventional style, which includes wearing makeup and feminine clothing, navigates the industry. Joc concluded that Playboi Carti's unorthodox style is a form of self-expression and an attempt to differentiate himself. Joc respects Playboi Carti for embracing his own creative perspective fostering a conversation about gender expression in hip-hop. Joc's daughter also shared her perspective, highlighting that many college students embrace similar styles. Joc concluded that judgment should be put aside to appreciate the innovative blending of fashion and music.
