The 2024 BET Hip Hop Awards take place tonight on Tuesday, October 15 at 8 p.m. The pre-recorded show will be available to stream for free on BET from its Las Vegas, Nevada location with Philo (free trial). Don’t miss the biggest names in hip hop including artists like Travis Scott, GloRilla and many more.
What can hip hop fans expect from this year’s show? Who is hosting and performing? Which stars will be honored? We’ll answer those questions and more as you get set to watch tonight’s event.
Grammy-nominated musician Fat Joe will host the 2024 BET Hip Hop Awards show this year.
This year, the BET Hip Hop Awards show is not being streamed live. That’s because the show was pre-recorded, and took place on October 8.
The 2024 edition of the BET Hip Hop Awards took place at Drai’s Beachclub & Nightclub in Las Vegas, Nevada.
What: 2024 BET Hip Hop Awards
When: Tuesday, October 15 at 8/7c
TV Channel: BET (You can also watch on other channels like BET HER, VH1, and MTV2)
How to watch streaming on demand, or online: You can watch the 2024 BET Hip Hop Awards LIVE for free with Philo (free trial) or with Fubo (free trial). If you’re out of free trials, you can sign up for Philo for only $28/month (cancel anytime, no contract).
This year’s performers include 2 Chainz, Big Boogie featuring GloRilla, Bossman DLow, E-40, Juicy J, Soulja Boy featuring Roscoe Dash, Trina featuring Yung Miami, 310babii and others. While these are the headliners, there could be some surprises. You can also see the full list of performers for tonight’s awards show on BET.com.
Travis Scott will receive the “I Am Hip Hop” award for his outstanding contribution to the genre.
