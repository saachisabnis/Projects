The Weeknd has shared the release date for his next album. Hurry Up Tomorrow is officially out January 24 31, serving “as the final chapter in his trilogy” that also includes 2020’s After Hours and 2022’s Dawn FM. An official tracklist has not yet been announced, but the Weeknd has recently released “Dancing in the Flames,” the Playboi Carti collaboration “Timeless,” and the Anitta-featuring “São Paulo.”
In September, the Weeknd revealed the titled for Hurry Up Tomorrow and played a special concert in São Paulo. To celebrate the album’s release, he’ll perform another one-off show, this time at Pasadena’s Rose Bowl, on Saturday, January 25.
