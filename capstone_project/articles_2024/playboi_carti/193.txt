Travis Scott was the musical guest on the new episode of Saturday Night Live. He did two songs from 2023’s Utopia, “My Eyes” and “Fe!n,” featuring Playboi Carti. Watch the performances below.
Scott and host Ramy Youssef also starred in a musical Please Don’t Destroy sketch. In the clip, the comedians gets too high during what’s supposed to be a fun night out with the rapper. Watch the sketch, as well as Scott’s episode promos with Youssef, below.
Ahead of his Saturday Night Live appearance, Scott released the Gabriel Moses–directed music video for “Fe!n.” Find that below, too.
