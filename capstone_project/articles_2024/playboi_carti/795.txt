Celebrities and fans are mourning the loss of Atlanta-born rapper Rich Homie Quan, who died at the age of 34. 
On Thursday (September 5), news broke that Rich Homie Quan died from an alleged drug overdose. 
The rapper's family told TMZ they are "shattered and heartbroken by his sudden death." A spokesperson from the Fulton County morgue confirmed to the outlet that RHQ died in his Atlanta home. No cause of death has been officially reported.
