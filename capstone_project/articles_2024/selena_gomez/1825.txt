OMG! ROSÉ’s collab with Bruno Mars “APT.” just SMASHED the Billboard Hot 100, debuting at #8!  That’s the highest EVER for any K-pop female artist – sis really did THAT! 
The track is giving everyone major feels, competing with heavy hitters like Billie Eilish (#3) and Sabrina Carpenter (#5) on this week’s chart! Talk about keeping some SERIOUS company! 
Fun fact: The previous record for K-pop queens? BLACKPINK’s “Ice Cream” with Selena Gomez at #13 back in 2020. Rosé just said “hold my champagne” and CRUSHED her own solo record (from “On The Ground” at #70) 
But wait, there’s MORE! This bop isn’t just slaying in the US – it hit #4 on the UK Official Singles Chart too! We see you, global queen! 
BTW… this is just a teaser for what’s coming! “APT.” is the pre-release single from Rosé’s upcoming debut solo album “rosie” dropping this December! We. Can’t. WAIT!
