The much-anticipated reboot of “Wizards Of Waverly Place” has gotten its first trailer! The new show, “Wizards Beyond Waverly Place” will star David Henrie as Justin Russo once again, as he takes in young wizard Billie, played by Janice LeAnn Brown, and helps train her. The fate of the world is in Billie’s hands…but she needs a lot of help. The trailer shows off a lot of magic hijinks, family bonding, and more. It also…shows that Selena Gomez makes a cameo at the very least! “Wizards Beyond Waverly Place” premieres on Disney Channel with two episodes on October 29th at 8PM Eastern. The first eight episodes will be availale on Disney+ the next day.
