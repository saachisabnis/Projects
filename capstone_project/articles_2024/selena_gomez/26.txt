Liam Payne’s girlfriend, Kate Cassidy, was left “devastated” after his sudden death, a source exclusively tells Us Weekly. “It hasn’t hit her yet. Kate and Liam had a strong relationship and she feels immense guilt leaving Argentina early and leaving him alone,” the insider says. Cassidy, 25, joined the former One Direction singer in Buenos Aires, Argentina, earlier this month, returning to the U.S. just two days before he died on October 16. Payne fell from the third-floor balcony of his hotel, and a partial autopsy later revealed that he had multiple drugs in his system at the time of his death. He was 31 years old.
