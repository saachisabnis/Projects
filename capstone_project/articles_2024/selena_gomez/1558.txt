What happens when two kids interview a movie star?
Find out when Lyla and Luna sit down with Danny DeVito for another latest installment of “Kid Gloves” the viral interview series where sisters Lyla and Luna Polowy roast Hollywood’s biggest celebrities (Jim Carrey, Ryan Reynolds, Selena Gomez, Snoop Dogg, Timothée Chalamet and many more).
In the latest episode, Lyla and Luna give living legend Danny DeVito “the business” as he promotes the release of his 1996 family favorite Matilda on 4K Blu-ray as well as his live presentation of the film with the New Jersey Symphony March 22.
