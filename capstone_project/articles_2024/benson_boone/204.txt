“One Thing Right” collaborators Kane Brown and Marshmello reunited for a new single, and it received a warm welcome at pop radio.
Entitled “Miles On It,” the new release landed at 67 Mediabase-monitored pop stations this week. It ranks as the format’s most added song.
Picked up by 59 stations, Shaboozey’s “A Bar Song (Tipsy)” ranks as second-most added. Gracie Abrams’ “Risk” follows in third on the Mediabase pop add board, courtesy of its 30 impact-week pickups.
Sabrina Carpenter’s “Espresso” follows in fourth with 22 new pickups, and an add count of 19 slots Chappell Roan’s “Good Luck, Babe!” in fifth.
This week’s other notable pop radio options: Ariana Grande’s “the boy is mine” (18 adds, 6th-most, tie), Tommy Richman’s “MILLION DOLLAR BABY” (18 adds, 6th-most, tie), Benson Boone’s “Slow It Down” (17 adds, 8th-most), Beyonce & Miley Cyrus’ “II MOST WANTED” (16 adds, 9th-most, tie), and Bibi & Jackson Wang’s “Feeling Lucky” (16 adds, 9th-most, tie).
