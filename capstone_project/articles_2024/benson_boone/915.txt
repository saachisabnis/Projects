Tyla’s got her style together in Cannes.
The South African singer took the stage at Spotify Beach during Cannes Lions on Tuesday in a pair of below the knee camo patchwork boots. The pointed toe shoes had a stiletto heel measuring approximately 4-inches and button detailing. The shoes were made out of a denim like material with button detailing and an uneven, rugged look at the top where the boot hit her leg.

Tyla completed her stylish look with a colorful bikini top and a cutout black one piece. She opted for a short camo printed miniskirt with a colorful string belt and bright yellow bikini bottoms poking out of her unbuttoned skirt. She wore colorful hoops in her ears with her hair done naturally.

She got the crowd on their feet during her performance, but walked the carpet solo before hitting the stage. The 22-year-old was also seen posing backstage with singer songwriter Melanie Fiona, Shaboozey and Janelle Monae. Benson Boone also performed during the night’s festivities.
The fashionable artist sported a pair of denim boots with buttons when she visited a pop-up store in Shoreditch on March 27 in London, England. She went with an over the knee mustard yellow rounded toe pair with a thick block heel in December on TikTok and a pointed toe black pair with a feather trim for a project with Elle in May.
The Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity is a global event in Cannes, France geared towards those working in creative communications, advertising and related fields. It is considered the largest gathering of the advertising and creative communications industry. This years’ festival was the 71st. It began June 17 and will end June 21 and also has an award show component.
