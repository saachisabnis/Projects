On the heels of earning the most added honor at pop radio, Justin Timberlake’s “No Angels” achieves the same feat at hot adult contemporary. The single landed at another 23 Mediabase-monitored Hot AC stations this week, earning first place on the add board.
A new playlist option for 19 Hot AC stations, Beyoncé & Miley Cyrus’ “II MOST WANTED” ranks as second-most added.
Dua Lipa’s “Illusion” takes third place with 13 pickups, while an add count of 12 positions Benson Boone’s “Slow It Down” in fourth on the Mediabase Hot AC add board.
Picked up by 10 more stations, Hozier’s “Too Sweet” ranks as fifth-most added.
