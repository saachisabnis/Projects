Benson Boone’s “Beautiful Things” continues its reign over two separate radio formats, notching a second week atop the Mediabase pop radio chart and a third week at #1 on the hot adult contemporary listing.
— “Beautiful Things” stays atop the pop chart courtesy of the ~16,837 spins it received during the April 21-27 tracking period (+270).
Teddy Swims’ “Lose Control” stays at #2, while Jack Harlow’s “Lovin On Me” retains its #3 ranking. Sabrina Carpenter’s “Feather” (#4, =) and Ariana Grande’s “we can’t be friends (wait for your love)” (#5, +2) complete the Top 5.
— The Benson Boone smash meanwhile garnered ~5,894 Hot AC spins during the tracking week (+107).
“Lose Control” also stays at #2 on the Hot AC chart, while Noah Kahan’s “Stick Season” ticks up one place to #3. Taylor Swift’s “Cruel Summer” rises one level to #4, and Tate McRae’s “greedy” ascends a level to #5.
