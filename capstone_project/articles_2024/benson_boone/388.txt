Beyoncé's "Texas Hold 'Em" continues its reign on the Billboard Hot 100 for a second consecutive week. The song experienced a surge in radio airplay and maintained strong streaming numbers, while also climbing the Adult Pop Airplay and Pop Airplay charts. Kanye West's track "Carnival" featuring Ty Dolla Sign, Rich The Kid, and Playboi Carti had a surge in popularity to land at No. 2.
Jack Harlow's “Lovin On Me” remains high on the chart at No. 3, followed by Benson Boone's “Beautiful Things” at No. 4. and Teddy Swims' "Lose Control at No. 5. SZA's "Saturn" debuted at number six on the Hot 100, becoming her 10th career top 10 hit. Zach Bryan's “I Remember Everything” landed at No. 7, Tate McRae's “Greedy” was No. 8, SZA's “Snooze” was No. 9 and Taylor Swift's "Cruel Summer" rounds out the top 10.
