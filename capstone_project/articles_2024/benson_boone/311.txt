Pop music star Taylor Swift's Tortured Poets Club is the No. 1 album in the United States this week.

               Coming in at No. 2 on the Billboard 200 chart dated Saturday is Future & Metro Boomin's We Don't Trust You, followed by Beyonce's Cowboy Carter at No. 3, Morgan Wallen's One Thing at a Time at No. 4 and Pearl Jam's Dark Matter at No. 5.
Rounding out the top tier are Future & Metro Boomin's We Still Don't Trust You at No. 6, Noah Kahan's Stick Season at No. 7, Benson Boone's Fireworks & Rollerblades at No. 8, SZA's SOS at No. 9 and Swift's Lover at No. 10.
