Mega pop star Taylor Swift is heading to London's Wembley Stadium in just a matter of days as she prepares to welcome thousands of fans.


  With sold-out days for all of her June dates and her extra dates in late August, Swift has wowed the world with her record-breaking Eras Tour.


  Celebrating the 'All Too Well' singers career and albums, the multi-Grammy winner will be performing hits from 'You Belong With Me' to her latest hits 'So High School' and many more through her three-hour show.


  But, fans lucky enough to get tickets to see Taylor Swift in Wembley won't just be seeing the mega pop star, as the singer confirmed her London support acts and they're very impressive.


  Find out who is supporting Swift on each night of her Eras Tour in Wembley this August.
