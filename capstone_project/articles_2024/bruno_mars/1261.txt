HOUSTON – Mark your calendars!
The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo revealed its 2025 concert genre calendar Thursday.
Recommended Videos
It shows which type of music will be featured each night of the rodeo. This gives music fans a sneak peek at possibilities for the highly-anticipated performer reveal in January.
The lineup will be officially announced on January 9, 2025 at 7 p.m.
Rodeo and concert tickets go on sale Jan. 16, 2025 at rodeohouston.com, from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m., starting at $25, plus a $4 per ticket convivence fee.
Season tickets are available now on the website.
The rodeo runs from March 4 through the 23rd.

Rodeo Houston organizes its lineup years in advance based on audience research, memberships and surveys.
Over the years, the event has set attendance records, making it one of the most celebrated annual events in Houston.
Some of the top-attended performances include:
1. Jonas Brothers – 75,600 (March 15, 2024)
2. Los Tigres Del Norte – 75,595 (March 10, 2024, Go Tejano Day)
3. Cardi B – 75,580 (March 1, 2019, Black Heritage Day)
4. Garth Brooks – 75,577 (March 18, 2018)
5. Calibre 50 – 75,565 (March 11, 2018, Go Tejano Day)
6. Siggno – 75,508 (March 19, 2017, Go Tejano Day)
7. Banda MS and Pesado – 75,177 (March 16, 2014, Go Tejano Day)
8. Maroon 5 – 75,078 (March 21, 2015)
9. Bruno Mars – 75,068 (March 9, 2019)
10. Ariana Grande – 75,033 (March 20, 2014)
With record-breaking crowds and unforgettable performances, Rodeo Houston continues to cement its place as Houston’s most popular event.
