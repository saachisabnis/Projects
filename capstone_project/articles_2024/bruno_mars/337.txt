DAYTON, Ohio (WDTN) — The Dayton Air Show parade, long a favorite part of the annual Vandalia event, is being replaced in 2025.
Introducing the Vandalia Flight Festival.
During the two nights of the free family event at the Vandalia Recreation Center, you can meet the pilots behind the U.S. Air Force Thunderbirds and other performers. It will feature activities for everyone as well as food trucks.
On Friday, June 20, the festival will be highlighted by a concert fronted by the Lt. Dan Band, fronted by actor Gary Sinise (“Forrest Gump,” “Apollo 13”).
“They play a little bit of everything, from Adele to Stevie Wonder to Bruno Mars.”said Ken Kreitzer, head of Dayton Air Show media relations.
The festival will also feature a concert on Saturday, June 21, with details to be revealed in the future.
Tickets are free, but you need to register for them at daytonairshow.com.
