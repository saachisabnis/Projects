LAS VEGAS, Nev. (FOX5) - FOX5 found out if American singer-songwriter Bruno Mars owes about $50 million to MGM.
Viral videos and discussions online say the 38-year-old has gambling debt with the giant casino.
FOX5′s Victoria Saha reached out to MGM, and they confirmed with us that the speculations are false.
“He has no debt with MGM,” MGM Resorts International said.
MGM says they are “proud” of their relationship with the singer. They say he’s “one of the world’s most thrilling and dynamic performers.”
“From his shows at Dolby Live at Park MGM to the new Pinky Ring lounge at Bellagio, Bruno’s brand of entertainment attracts visitors from around the globe. MGM and Bruno’s partnership is longstanding and rooted in mutual respect,” MGM says. “Together, we are excited to continue creating unforgettable experiences for our guests.”
