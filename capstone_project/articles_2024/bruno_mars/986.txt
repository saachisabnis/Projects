Get ready to throw some Versace on the floor this April as Grammy Award-winning singer Bruno Mars will return to Singapore as part of his new tour.
Mars will be taking the stage at National Stadium on Apr 5 and 6, performing a bevy of his famous hits. The second show was added, following "incredible demand". 
A post shared by Live Nation SG (@livenationsg)
Tickets for both shows will go on sale at 11am on Saturday (Jan 20) via Ticketmaster, with prices starting at S$108. 
Bruno Mars is one of the best-selling artistes of all time, having sold over 200 million singles worldwide. He has won 15 Grammy Awards, with his most recent ones being Record Of The Year and Best R&B Performance for the hit track Leave The Door Open.
