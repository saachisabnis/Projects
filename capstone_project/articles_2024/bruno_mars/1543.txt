Lady Gaga is getting into the Christmas spirit as she has released a cover of "Santa Claus Is Comin’ to Town".
The track was released to coincide with Lady Gaga's appearance on the Apple TV+ special A Carpool Karaoke Christmas. "Santa Claus Is Comin' to Town" follows "Disease", which was the first preview of new music from her seventh album; the follow-up to 2020's, Chromatica. The track was released after her standalone collaboration with Bruno Mars, “Die With a Smile.”
A Carpool Karaoke Christmas will be released globally on 20 December.
