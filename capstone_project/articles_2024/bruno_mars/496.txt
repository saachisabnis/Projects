CAPE GIRARDEAU, Mo. (KFVS) - The Southeast Missouri State University Marching Band and Wind Symphony teamed up to perform together for “Bandapalooza.”
The Bedell Performance Hall hosted the event at 3 p.m. on Sunday, November 17.
According to the event’s Facebook page, the performance featured all the full sights and sounds of the nationally recognized SEMO Pride of the Heartland Marching Band, along with the Southeast Wind Symphony.
The program included the Missouri premiere of Jacob Evarts’ An Opening Fanfare, Daniel Freiberg’s Latin American Chronicles featuring Michael Chapa on soprano saxophone, Deciduous by Viet Cuong, Piazzolla’s Libertango featuring Christopher Mosher on trumpet, songs from Bruno Mars, Patrick Dunnigan, and much more.
The Southeast Missouri State University Band Program includes the Southeast Wind Symphony, Marching Band, Concert Band, and Show Band.
