MARSHALL, Texas (KTAL/KMSS)—In October, a new live show will bring legendary soul music classics to Memorial City Hall.
Uptown: A Celebration of Motown & Soul will perform in Marshall on Saturday, October 18, at 7:30 p.m.

The show is described as “soulful vibes of R&B fused with today’s hits.” UPTOWN is a three-man group from New York City. The vocalists blend contemporary radio hits with classics of the Motown sound and are frequently compared to music icons like Stevie Wonder and Marvin Gaye.
The group will perform tunes from The Four Tops, Al Green, Earth Wind & Fire, Prince, and Bruno Mars and dynamic choreography. Online tickets are only available via the Memorial City Hall website box office. Memorial City Hall does not work with third-party ticket sellers like Stub Hub, Vivid Seats, or others.
