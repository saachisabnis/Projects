Every Thursday evening starting on June 27th through July, the city of Lake Elsinore will be hosting free concerts, the city announced Saturday.
The second annual “Live at the Lakefront” summer concert series will begin every Thursday at 7 p.m. at 32040 Riverside Drive.

The June 27th concert called “Uptown Funk” is a tribute to Bruno Mars, while the July 11th concert called “Joyas Prestadas” is a tribute to the late Regional Mexican singer Jenni Rivera.
On July 18, the concert “Cheez Whiz” will be a tribute to the 80s and 90s, and the last concert, on July 25, will be a performance by Banda Imperio Sinaloense.
A variety of food vendors will be at the concert, the city announced.
The event is free along with free parking. The city advised guests to bring lawn chairs and blankets. Ice chests are allowed, but the public was reminded to maintain a clean and safe environment.
