Sabrina Carpenter has been announced as a performer at BST Hyde Park on 5 July 2025. Other names already announced for the seven day festival includes Olivia Rodrigo, Jeff Lynne's ELO and Noah Kahan.
This announcement comes after a big year for the 25-year-old singer, who recently received a whole load of Grammy nominations including Song of the Year, Record of the Year, Album of the Year and Best New Artist.
Her newest album 'Short n' Sweet', which was released in August this year, topped the chart in 19 countries, and she has a Netflix special called 'A Nonsense Christmas'.

American Express presents BST Hyde Park spans across seven days in summer between 27 June and 13 July. The Amex presale is currently taking place, and then tickets go on general sale at 10 am on Wednesday 11 December.
Last year, headliners for the London festival included SZA, Kings of Leon, Kylie Minogue and Shania Twain. In past years, Hyde Park has hosted legends such as Billy Joel, Lana Del Rey and Elton John.
Tickets to Sabrina's show go on general sale at 10am GMT on Wednesday 11 December on Ticketmaster.
