For a fourth time Taylor Swift won the Grammy for album of the year, a new record. At 34, she has moved ahead of music industry titans Frank Sinatra, Paul Simon and Stevie Wonder, who all won the prize three times.
Midnights, Swift’s most recent collection of new material, was recognised over competition from the likes of Olivia Rodrigo, Lana Del Rey and Miley Cyrus.
