If your musical tastes run towards piano-playing women, this is the weekend for you in the metropolitan Detroit area.
• Pop sensation Olivia Rodrigo brings her Guts World Tour to town at 7 p.m. Saturday, March 23, at Detroit’s Little Caesars Arena, supporting her chart-topping second album, “Guts,” the six Top 10 hits she’s logged so far. Chappell Roan opens. 2645 Woodward Ave., Detroit. 313-471-7000 or 313Presents.com.
• Celebrated Chinese pianist Yuja Wang plays Beethoven with the Detroit Symphony Orchestra through Saturday, March 23, at Orchestra Hall, 3711 Woodward Ave., Detroit. DSO Musical Director Jader Bignamini will be conducting, and Saturday’s 8 p.m. performance will be streamed free as part of the “Live From Orchestra Hall” series. 313-576-5111 or dso.org.
• Actress Alicia Witt (“Dune,” “Twin Peaks,” “Vanilla Sky”) also has plenty of music to her credit, including last year’s EP “Witness,” and she’ll play it at 8 p.m. Friday, March 22 at The Ark, 316 S. Main St., 734-761-1818 or theark.org.
