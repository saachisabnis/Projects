Olivia Rodrigo has recreated Nicole Kidman's AMC Theaters Ad at a recent concert in Montreal. 
According to Rolling Stone, in a TikTok filmed at Olivia's show, she reimagined Kidman's 2021 ad that was also parodied on Saturday Night Live. 
The 26-second social media clip is shot from several different angles as she wears a shirt that says, "We come to this place for magic."
Kidman reposted the video on her Instagram Story, writing, "You just won me some major points with my girls! Adore you, Olivia Rodrigo."
