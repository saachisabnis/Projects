DENVER (KDVR) — Grammy Award-winning rapper Megan Thee Stallion will make a stop in Denver during her “Hot Girl Summer Tour.”
She will perform on June 17 at Ball Arena and will be joined by rapper GloRilla.
 Full list of concerts at Ball Arena in 2024, so far 
The tour, which features stops in both the U.S. and internationally, starts in May and ends in late July.
This will be the 29-year-old artist’s first headlining tour, but it isn’t the first time she has performed at Ball Arena.
She previously performed on Dua Lipa’s “Future Nostalgia Tour” and performed in Denver in March 2022.
Ticket pre-sale starts Wednesday at 1 p.m. MDT, with the general sale starting Friday at 10 a.m.
Ball Arena will be star-studded this summer, with several other iconic performers lined up, with nearly every genre represented.
