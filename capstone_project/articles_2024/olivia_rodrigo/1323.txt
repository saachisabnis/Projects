Well good for you, fans of Olivia Rodrigo. On Thursday (May 9), the American songstress announced the Asian and Australian legs of her ongoing Guts world tour – and Singapore made the cut. Get ready to belt alongside Rodrigo as she takes the stage at the Singapore Indoor Stadium on Oct 1.
According to Live Nation Asia, there will be two presale sessions for Olivia Rodrigo's concert. The first will be for American Express cardholders from 10am on May 13. The second presale will be held for Live Nation members on May 15 from 12pm to 11.59pm.
General sales will then take place from 12pm on May 16 via Ticketmaster. 
Born in 2003, Olivia Rodrigo first gained popularity as one of the main stars on the Disney+ series High School Musical: The Musical: The Series. However, her singing career took off at a meteoric pace, following the runaway success of her debut album Sour.
The album produced the hit songs Drivers License, Deja Vu and Good 4 U, and was certified quadruple platinum in June 2023 after selling over 4 million units in the US.
In 2023, Rodrigo released her follow-up album Guts which was nominated for album of the year and best pop vocal album at the 66th Grammy Awards.
