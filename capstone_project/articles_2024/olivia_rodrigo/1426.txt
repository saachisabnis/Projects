Travis Scott, Luke Combs, and Burna Boy are the latest artists to join the list of performers for the Grammy Awards, joining the previously announced Olivia Rodrigo, Dua Lipa, and Billie Eilish. All six are also nominees in various categories this year. The list of performers will continue to grow in the two weeks leading up to the ceremony on February 4th. SZA leads the nominations this year with nine, while Eilish and Rodrigo each nabbed six. Trevor Noah will return to host, while Donna Summer, Gladys Knight, N.W.A., and Tammy Wynette are among the Lifetime Achievement Award honorees.
