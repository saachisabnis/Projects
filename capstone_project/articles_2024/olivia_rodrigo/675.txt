Will we be hearing new beabadoobee music in 2025?
In a Facebook post, the “Beaches” artist shared a photo of herself wearing headphones, alongside the caption, “I am listening to new music rn.”
Beabadoobee put out her latest album, This Is How Tomorrow Moves, in August. The Rick Rubin-produced record spawned the single “Beaches,” which sits in the top 10 on Billboard‘s Alternative Airplay chart.
Beabadoobee’s 2024 also included being named to the Time 100 Next list. Her 2025 so far includes performing at Coachella and opening shows for Olivia Rodrigo and Sabrina Carpenter.
