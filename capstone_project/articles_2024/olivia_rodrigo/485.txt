After a record-breaking 2023 at The O2, with over 2.5 million arena tickets sold, the world’s most popular live entertainment, leisure and retail destination is looking ahead to a stellar 2024.


  From comedy to country, rock to rap, and pop to podcasts, there’s something for everyone at The O2.


  Here’s 24 iconic shows to look forward to in 2024, at London’s world-famous venue, the O2:


  1. Jay Chou – The King of Mandopop, Jay Chou will be headlining The O2 for two nights in January 2024.


  2. Bring Me the Horizon - The BRIT and Grammy-nominated, multi-platinum quintet are set to rock out to audiences at The O2 with two sold out shows on Saturday, January 20 and Sunday, January 21, 2024.


  3. Depeche Mode - Fans will get to experience the raw, emotive power of the Memento Mori Tour in January 2024, as Depeche Mode headline The O2 for two shows.


  4. Davido - Global afrobeat superstar Davido returns to The O2 in January 2024 for a headline performance following the release of his ‘Timeless’ Album.


  5. D-Block Europe - Platinum-selling South London collective, D-Block Europe will return to The O2 for four nights in February.


  6. Strictly Come Dancing The Live Tour – Fan favourite Strictly Come Dancing is set to return to The O2 in February 2024, with an electrifying spectacle full of glitz, glamour, and pure entertainment.


  7. The 1975 - Bringing their ‘Still… At Their Very Best’ tour to The O2, these four shows will be hosted as the world’s first carbon-removed arena events.


  8. The BRIT Awards - The biggest night in British music returns to The O2, with The BRIT Awards 2024 with Mastercard taking place on Saturday, March 2.


  9. C2C: Country 2 Country - Get ready for three days of country music with headline performances from Kane Brown, Brad Paisley, and Old Dominion across the weekend of Friday 8 to Sunday 10 March 2024.


  More acts still to be announced.


  10. Ne-Yo - Three-time GRAMMY® Award-winning hitmaker Ne-Yo will be bringing his Champagne and Roses tour to The O2 with special guest Mario in March 2024.


  11. John Mayer - Following the tremendous success of his acoustic tour ‘Solo’, John Mayer will be headlining a show at The O2 on Monday 18 & Tuesday 19 March 2024.


  12. Pendulum – Live Drum & Bass act Pendulum are set to headline The O2 over the Easter weekend, following the success of Drum & Bass act Andy C at the venue in 2023.


  13. Michael McIntyre - Following his five-year tour hiatus, superstar comedian Michael McIntyre will be headlining five nights at The O2 as part of his brand-new show ‘Macnificent’ in April 2024.


  14. Take That - Iconic British band Take That make their return to The O2 for a six-night residency with special guest, Olly Murs in April 2024.


  15. Olivia Rodrigo - Global Pop superstar, Olivia Rodrigo will be headlining The O2 for the first time as part of her GUTS world tour in May 2024, with four very special shows.


  16. Shxts ‘n’ Gigs - Hit comedy podcast duo ShxtsNGigs will be headlining The O2 on Sunday 26 May 2024.


  17. Liam Gallagher - The O2 welcomes back the legendary Liam Gallagher for an incredible four-night residency celebrating 30 years of the seminal album ‘Definitely Maybe’ in June 2024.


  18. Karol G - Karol G is set to headline The O2 as part of the MAÑANA SERÁ BONITO tour, with two headline shows in June 2024.


  19. Girls Aloud - Legendary British-pop band Girls Aloud bring the Sound of the Underground to North Greenwich after their decade long hiatus, with five headline shows at The O2 in June 2024.


  20. The Killers - The Killers are bringing their ‘Rebel Diamonds’ tour to The O2 for six nights at The O2 in July 2024.


  21. 2024 USA Basketball Showcase - The USA Basketball Men’s national team will be playing at The O2 on Saturday 20 and Sunday 21 July 2024 in preparation for the Olympic Summer Games.


  22. Arijit Singh - Bollywood superstar Arijit Singh is set to headline The O2 in August 2024.


  23. Chris Stapleton - Country superstar Chris Stapleton will be bringing his ‘All-American Road Show’ to The O2 in October 2024.


  24. League of Legends – One of the largest and most popular gaming and sporting events in the world, the 2024 League of Legends World Championship Finals will be coming to The O2 in November 2024.
