Tyler, the Creator is spending his third consecutive week at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 with CHROMAKOPIA.
His latest studio effort earned 104,000 equivalent album units in its second week, including 76,000 in streaming equivalent album units (106.87 million on-demand streams of the songs), 28,000 in album sales and the remaining in track equivalent album units.
Meanwhile, debuting in this week’s top 10 is TOMORROW X TOGETHER at No. 2 with The Star Chapter: SANCTUARY. The record earned 98,000 equivalent album units in its first week including 95,500 in album sales, 2,500 in streaming equivalent album units and the rest in track equivalent album units.
Elsewhere on the chart are Sabrina Carpenter, Gracie Abrams and Billie Eilish from Nos. 3 to 5, followed by Chappell Roan at No. 6, Morgan Wallen at No. 7, Taylor Swift at No. 8, Rod Wave at No. 9 and Noah Kahan at No. 10.
