A NEW YEAR in 2025 is not too far away, and with it, there will be a whole new programme of events to enjoy at theatres such as the Bournemouth Pavilion.


  There is plenty to choose from, so we've highlighted all the musicians, comedians, stage shows and more that will be on offer there.


  To make sure you know who's coming and what you want to get tickets to we've listed them all for you by month.
