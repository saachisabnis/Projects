Sabrina Carpenter attended Louis Vuitton‘s summer 2025 Menswear show during Paris Fashion Week on  Tuesday in Paris. Other notable attendees included Burna Boy, Jackson Wang, Michael Fassbender, Joel Edgerton, Pusha T, Soo Joo Park, Anderson .Paak, Shygirl and Colman Domingo, among others.
Sat front row, the “Please Please Please” singer sported a pair of stark white pumps, likely from Louis Vuitton. The star’s footwear featured glossy white leather uppers, arched soles and 2 to 3-inch stiletto heels that lifted Carpenter to new heights. The style coordinated with the performer’s ensemble.

 









Carpenter is just one of many celebrities who favor classic pump styles. The heels are a go-to item, given their classic and sophisticated silhouette and height-boosting abilities. Depending on the colorway, the style also goes with just about everything, making it the perfect all-arounder. The closet staple is favored by many, including the likes of Venus Williams, Keke Palmer, Lily Allen, Eva Longoria and Kate Middleton, just to name a few.

Matching her footwear, the “Sue Me” singer wore a white blazer-inspired mini dress. The garment featured structural shoulders and a sparkling brooch fastened to the bodice. Layered underneath, Carpenter wore a white button-down fitted with a black Louis Vuitton tie featuring logo detailing scrawled across the front in white. Rounding out her look, the musician toted a white bowling bag with embossed Louis Vuitton detailing.

Carpenter’s shoe rotation tends to be sleek and whimsical. The “Almost Love” singer frequently wears platform pumps, sandals and boots with towering heels from brands including Valentino, Stuart Weitzman, and Naked Wolfe.
