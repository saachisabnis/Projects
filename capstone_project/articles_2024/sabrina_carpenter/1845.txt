Noah Kahan’s “Stick Season” officially reaches #1 on the hot adult contemporary radio format.
Played ~5,723 times during the May 12-18 tracking period, “Stick Season” ascends two places to claim #1 on the Mediabase Hot AC chart. This week’s spin count tops last week’s mark by 206.
Down one place, Benson Boone’s “Beautiful Things” settles for #2. Teddy Swims’ “Lose Control” slides one spot to #3, while Sabrina Carpenter’s “Feather” spends another week at #4.
Taylor Swift’s “Fortnight (featuring Post Malone)” again closes the Top 5, spending another week in the chart’s #5 position.
