Feid gave fans everything they wanted and more during his three consecutive sold-out shows at Medellín’s Estadio Atanasio Girardot in Colombia. The acclaimed performer invited a series of special guests, including Bad Bunny, Rauw Alejandro, Maluma, J Balvin, and more.
The artist had a sweet moment with his romantic partner Karol G after the 'Mañana Será Bonito' singer took the stage and dedicated her fan-favorite song 'Mi Ex Tenía Razón' to Feid, which has been rumored to be about him following her breakup from Anuel AA.
"I can't believe I'm singing this song to you," she said before her performance. The two artists shared a romantic embrace, with Karol G wearing a neon green ensemble in support of Feid.
He also invited Myke Towers, Tainy, Majid Jordan, Sech, and Yandel, surpassing 210,000 tickets sold across the three nights of shows at the biggest arena in Medellín, Feid cemented himself as an impactful global star. The shows also earned Feid a spot in the Guinness World Records for Furthest Distance Covered in a Musical Performance on Stage (10.3km).
Feid has made incredible friendships throughout his career, including Bad Bunny, who was the most-awaited guest, as online users started rumors about a potential performance from the Puerto Rican singer. He is also going strong in his relationship with Karol G after he was spotted supporting the artist during her successful tour around the globe.
