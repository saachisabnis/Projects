Dominik Mysterio says he’s down for a match between himself and Bad Bunny.
Bad Bunny was last seen at the 2023 WWE Backlash pay-per-view event in Puerto Rico. At that show, he faced off against Damian Priest in a Street Fight.
Speaking on a recent edition of “The Ringer Wrestling Show,” Dirty Dom commented on a possible match between himself and the rapper. He said,
“I know Bad Bunny did his thing with Damian in PR, I know he got involved in my match last year. He’s done a lot for the culture and what not, but at the end of the day, Damian said it — you’re a singer, dude. Stay in your lane. This is our world here, Judgment Day takes over here.”
He continued, “As far as unfinished business goes, if he’s more than willing to get in my face again, I’m down to throw down if you know what I’m talking about.”
