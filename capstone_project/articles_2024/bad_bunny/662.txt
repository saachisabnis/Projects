Bad Bunny is joining the cast of Darren Aronofsky’s film Caught Stealing, with Zoë Kravitz and Austin Butler. The thriller is based on Charlie Huston’s novel about an ex-baseball player caught in the underworld of 1990s NYC.
Brittany Cartwright, known for Vanderpump Rules and The Valley, has filed for divorce from Jax Taylor after five years of marriage. She cited “irreconcilable differences” and wants primary custody of their 3-year-old son.
Michael Crichton’s estate is suing the producers of the Max medical drama The Pitt for walking away from negotiations to create a new ER reboot that later became The Pitt. Noah Wyle stars as a new character in a new hospital.
RuPaul’s Drag Race UK season 6 guest judges will include Simon Le Bon, Alison Goldfrapp, Mabel, and others. They join regular judges RuPaul, Michelle Visage, Alan Carr, and Graham Norton. The premiere date is still TBA.
Kelly Monaco, who has played Samantha McCall on General Hospital since 2003, is leaving the show after nearly 2,300 episodes. Her character will be “killed off.” Monaco has been nominated for a Daytime Emmy for her work.
Professional wrestler Sid Vicious, also known as Sid Justice and Sycho Sid, has passed away at 63 after battling cancer. He was a prominent figure in 1990s wrestling, known for his imposing presence in both the WWF and WCW.
Michael Madsen’s domestic violence case, involving accusations of shoving his wife, DeAnna Madsen, was dismissed due to insufficient evidence. “We are thrilled that Michael Madsen is able to put this behind him,” his rep said.
Francis Ford Coppola says he intentionally cast “canceled” actors (like Jon Voight and Shia LaBeouf) for his self-funded sci-fi epic Megalopolis, because he didn’t want the controversial movie to be a “woke Hollywood production.”
During a Cirque du Soleil show in Oregon, an aerial hoop artist fell while performing in KOOZA. The incident, captured on video, sparked gasps from the shocked audience. The performer is recovering and receiving medical care.
