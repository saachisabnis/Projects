NEW ORLEANS (WVUE) - The Caesar’s Superdome turned pink on Wednesday (May 8) night as Nicki Minaj brought her “Pink Friday 2 World Tour” to New Orleans. 
The pop superstar’s arrival in the city was marked by a special tribute, with the Superdome, located next door to the Smoothie King Center, illuminated in pink to honor her visit.
Minaj, known for her bold style and chart-topping hits, drew thousands of fans to her Wednesday night concert. 
The rapper thanked the crowd for attending, stating, “You could be anywhere else but you chose to be here with us.”
Minaj’s performance followed an electric concert by Bad Bunny the night before in the SKC.
