One of the biggest movies of the July 4 weekend this year isn’t playing in theaters; it’s going straight to Netflix.
That’s Beverly Hills Cop: Axel F, the first movie in the popular Eddie Murphy series in 30 years. Murphy returns as Axel, and Axel returns to Beverly Hills for yet another fish-out-of-water adventure. (Ladies and gentlemen: Hide your tailpipes.)
There are dozens of other new titles, of course. Cobra Kai will return for its final season in July, and Netflix is also debuting an Exploding Kittens animated series as well. You’ll also get a new docuseries about real police officers from Law & Order creator Dick Wolf, the festival hit Skywalkers: A Love Story, and the streamer’s new take on The Decameron.
