As the world gears up for summer 2024, music lovers are in for a treat with an array of music festivals that span genres, continents, and atmospheres. 
Whether you’re looking to dance under the desert sky in California, immerse yourself in the lush forests of Michigan, or groove by the beaches of Spain, there’s something for every musical taste and adventure-seeking spirit. 
Get ready to mark your calendars and pack your bags for an unforgettable summer soundtrack. 
Here’s your ultimate guide to some of the most anticipated music festivals happening this summer. You’ll get all the information you need, just right here!
Let’s get started!
Dates: April 12-14 and April 19-21, 2024Location: Indio, California, USA
Kicking off the summer festival season, Coachella is a mecca for music and art enthusiasts. Known for its eclectic mix of genres, Coachella 2024 promises a lineup that includes the biggest names in music. 
Expect headliners like Billie Eilish, Bad Bunny, and BLACKPINK, who are set to deliver unforgettable performances. Beyond the music, Coachella offers a vibrant display of art installations, gourmet food vendors, and an unparalleled social scene, making it a holistic cultural experience.
Dates: June 26-30, 2024Location: Pilton, Somerset, England
Glastonbury is the quintessential British music festival, renowned for its vast array of stages and genres, from rock and pop to electronic and folk. This year, the Pyramid Stage will feature legends like Elton John, who is making his final UK festival appearance, alongside contemporary stars like Taylor Swift and Kendrick Lamar. 
The festival is also famous for its non-musical attractions, including theatre, circus, and healing fields, providing a unique and immersive experience.

Dates: June 13-16, 2024Location: Manchester, Tennessee, USA
Bonnaroo stands out for its diverse lineup and community spirit. This year’s headliners include Foo Fighters, Lizzo, and Tame Impala. 
Located on a 700-acre farm, Bonnaroo offers more than just music, with activities like yoga classes, a water park, and various art installations. 
The festival’s “Bonnaroovian Code” emphasizes respect, positivity, and environmental consciousness, fostering a welcoming atmosphere for all attendees.
Dates: August 1-4, 2024Location: Grant Park, Chicago, Illinois, USA
Lollapalooza is a staple in the American festival circuit, known for its multi-genre lineup and urban setting. This year’s festival will feature performances by Post Malone, Dua Lipa, and Red Hot Chili Peppers. 
The festival also includes a Kidzapalooza section for families, as well as an array of food vendors offering international cuisine. 
Lollapalooza’s location in the heart of Chicago allows festival-goers to explore the city’s vibrant cultural scene between sets.
Dates: July 19-21 and July 26-28, 2024Location: Boom, Belgium
Tomorrowland is the ultimate destination for electronic dance music (EDM) fans. The festival is renowned for its elaborate stage designs and immersive themes. 
This year’s lineup includes top DJs such as Martin Garrix, Armin van Buuren, and David Guetta. Tomorrowland is more than just a music festival; it’s a visual and auditory spectacle that transports attendees to a fantastical world, complete with pyrotechnics and synchronized light shows.
Dates: June 29 – July 6, 2024Location: Roskilde, Denmark
Roskilde is one of Europe’s longest-running music festivals, known for its progressive lineup and commitment to social causes. This year’s headliners include The Weeknd, Florence + The Machine, and Travis Scott. 
The festival donates all profits to humanitarian and cultural charities, embodying its motto, “Music, Art, and Activism.” With a rich blend of music, art installations, and debates, Roskilde offers a comprehensive cultural experience.
Dates: July 26-28, 2024Location: Naeba Ski Resort, Niigata Prefecture, Japan
Set against the stunning backdrop of the Japanese Alps, Fuji Rock is Asia’s premier music festival. This year’s lineup features Radiohead, Björk, and The Chemical Brothers. Known for its eco-friendly initiatives, Fuji Rock promotes sustainability through recycling programs and the use of green energy. 
The festival also offers various outdoor activities, including hiking and hot springs, making it a perfect blend of music and nature.
Dates: June 5-9, 2024Location: Barcelona, Spain
Primavera Sound is celebrated for its cutting-edge lineup that spans indie rock, electronic, and hip-hop. This year’s festival features headliners like Kendrick Lamar, Rosalía, and The Strokes. 
Located at the Parc del Fòrum, the festival offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. Primavera Sound is also known for its commitment to gender equality, striving for a 50/50 gender balance in its lineup.
Dates: July 4-7, 2024Location: Werchter, Belgium
Rock Werchter is one of Europe’s largest rock festivals, attracting a diverse crowd with its varied lineup. This year, the festival will feature acts like Pearl Jam, Imagine Dragons, and The Killers. 
Known for its well-organized setup and high-quality facilities, Rock Werchter ensures a comfortable experience for all attendees. The festival’s location near Brussels allows for easy access to the city’s attractions.
Dates: July 4-7, 2024Location: New Orleans, Louisiana, USA
The Essence Festival celebrates Black culture and music, featuring a mix of hip-hop, R&B, and soul. This year’s headliners include Beyoncé, Bruno Mars, and Megan Thee Stallion. Beyond the music, the festival offers empowerment seminars, beauty and wellness expos, and a marketplace for Black-owned businesses. 
Held in the vibrant city of New Orleans, the festival is a cultural extravaganza that extends beyond the music to embrace the city’s rich heritage.
