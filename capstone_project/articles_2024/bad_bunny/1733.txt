"Happy Gilmore 2" brought some Hollywood excitement to Hackensack on Wednesday, Nov. 20.
Adam Sandler films in Hackensack and Bogota.
Sources confirmed that the filming took place near Atlantic and State Streets (have photos? email clevine@dailyvoice.com). 
Residents also reported seeing film crews at HacPac and Riverview Liquors in Bogota.
The highly anticipated sequel, starring Adam Sandler as Happy Gilmore and Christopher McDonald as his unforgettable rival Shooter McGavin, has been shooting across New Jersey, with stops in Hackettstown, Kearny, Millburn, Morristown, Verona, and now Hackensack.
The movie, set to stream on Netflix, began production on Monday, Sept. 9. Julie Bowen is reprising her role as Happy's love interest, while new faces include Bad Bunny and Kansas City Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce, who is rumored to make an appearance.
Fans of the cult classic can expect a fresh take on the comedic rivalry that made the original film a hit. Stay tuned for more updates on this star-studded production!
