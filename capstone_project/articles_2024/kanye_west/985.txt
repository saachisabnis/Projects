Earlier this month, Pharrell enlisted the talents of LeBron James for his latest Louis Vuitton campaign. At the start of January, the NBA superstar was officially unveiled as the face of the French luxury fashion house’s Men’s Spring/Summer 2024 collection.
In photos and videos shared on social media, the LA Lakers superstar could be seen modeling a variety of garments and bags while strolling through city streets.
“LOUIS VUITTON BRON! LV Men’s Spring-Summer 2024 coming soon! LET’S GO!!!! [fire emoji, raised hands emoji],” the four-time NBA Champion wrote in an Instagram post.
