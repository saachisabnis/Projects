 The comic book empires Marvel and DC have lost their long-held trademark for the term “super hero,” making it free for everyone to use. The change came after a legal challenge from the creators of the comic series Superbabies, who argued that no one should own the term. The Superbabies lawyers won. So now, the term “super hero” is in the public domain, opening up new possibilities for storytellers everywhere.
