Per HipHopDX, Twista's iconic track "Slow Jamz" finally received platinum certification, just a few days after the song's 20th anniversary. On January 25th, the RIAA officially certified the song as platinum. Released on November 10, 2003, "Slow Jamz" featured Kanye West and Jamie Foxx, reaching No.1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and earning a Grammy nomination in 2005 for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration.
