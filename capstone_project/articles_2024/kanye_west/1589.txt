Elliott Wilson analyzed the current hip-hop media landscape, discussing influential figures like N.O.R.E., Gillie & Wallo, Lil Yachty, Ebro, Nardwuar, and others featured on Complex's Media Power List. He highlighted Angela Yee’s impactful transition from "The Breakfast Club" to her own show, commending its promising start and her ability to draw notable guests. Wilson also praised Big Boy, arguing he’s underrated at 16th due to his high-profile interviews, including with Kendrick Lamar and Kanye West. Angie Martinez's successful pivot to podcasting, maintaining iconic status even after moving from Hot 97, was another focal point. He acknowledged Bootleg Kev’s consistency and rise in the media landscape, finally earning him a rightful spot on the list.
