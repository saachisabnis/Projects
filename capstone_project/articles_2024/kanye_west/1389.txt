Kanye West and Ty Dolla $ign‘s sophomore joint album VULTURES Volume 2 is finally here, one day after the new planned release date.
Clocking in at approximately 50 minutes, the 16-track album arrives almost three months after it was initially scheduled to release in May. Volume 2 features guest appearances from Don Toliver, Kodak Black, Playboi Carti, Future, Lil Wayne, his children Chicago and North West, Charlie Wilson, The Inter Milan Ultras, Young Thug, Lil Durk, Lil Baby, James Blake, 070 Shake, CyHi the Prynce, Desiigner and, in a fun twist, Todd Rundgren.  Meanwhile, its album cover sees Ty pay homage to his incarcerated brother Jabreal Muhammad, also known as Big TC. Muhammad is currently serving a life sentence for a murder both the brothers maintain he didn’t commit.
Stream Ye and Ty Dolla $ign’s VULTURES Volume 2 on Spotify and Apple Music.
