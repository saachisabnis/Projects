Teddy Swims has secured his first ever No. 1 hit on the Billboard Hot 100 chart with his song "Lose Control." The track took 32 weeks to reach the top spot, making it the longest ascent to No. 1 for a solo male artist in the chart's 65-year history.
Benson Boone’s “Beautiful Things” rose to No. 2 this week, Ariana Grande’s “We Can’t Be Friends (Wait for Your Love)” drops to No. 3, Jack Harlow’s “Lovin on Me” was back up to No. 4 and “Carnival,” by Ye and Ty Dolla $ign rounds out the top 5. Cardi B's "Enough (Miami)" also debuted at No. 9, giving her a total of 12 top 10 hits.
