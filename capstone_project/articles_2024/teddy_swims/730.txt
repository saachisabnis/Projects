AUSTIN – Austin City Limits has released its star-studded lineup for this year’s music festival, with tickets going on sale Tuesday.
ACL weekend one will be held Oct. 4-6, with weekend two following Oct. 11-13 in Austin’s Zilker Park.
Recommended Videos
Headliners for ACL include Dua Lipa, Tyler the Creator, Chris Stapleton, Blink 182, Sturgill Simpson, Pretty Lights, Khruangbin and Leon Bridges.
Other favorites in the lineup include Renee Rapp from 2024′s “Mean Girls,” Tyla, Norah Jones, Chappel Roan, Orville Peck and Teddy Swims, among various artists. (See poster below.)
Three-day tickets for both ACL weekends will go on sale at noon on Tuesday.
