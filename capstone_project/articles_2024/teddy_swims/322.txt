WILMINGTON, N.C. (WECT) - Teddy Swims is set to play Live Oak Bank Pavilion on Wednesday, May 14, 2025, according to an announcement from the music venue.
Freak Freely is scheduled to perform as well.
“Wilmington! Teddy Swims can’t wait to see y’all at Live Oak Bank Pavilion Wilmington on 5/14,” the pavilion says in the announcement.
Tickets will first become available at 8 a.m. Wednesday, Oct. 2, during the show’s presale. Tickets can be purchased online here.
