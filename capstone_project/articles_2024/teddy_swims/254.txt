Her feet were adorned with Timberland boots in the classic style. This all-season boot, inspired by the original waterproof design, offers the wearer both tireless waterproof performance and the instantly recognizable styling of a work boot. It features 200 grams of warm, down-free PrimaLoft® insulation, a padded collar for ankle comfort, and a rubber lug outsole for traction.
Timberland, established in 1973 by the Abington Shoe Company, gained popularity in the 1990s hip-hop scene with its iconic 6-inch boot. Originally a work boot, Timberland is now a leading outdoor brand known for its versatile footwear and apparel.
