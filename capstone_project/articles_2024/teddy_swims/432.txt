ROCKFORD, Ill. (WIFR) - Hard Rock Casino Rockford announces genre-blending artist Teddy Swims is set to take the stage on Friday, Sept. 20. 
Doors open for the show at 7 p.m. with the show expected to begin at 8 p.m. Audience members must be 21 or older to enter. 
Hard Rock Rockford leaders say due to popular demand, pre-sale tickets for the show sold out. Tickets will go on sale for the public on Friday, Aug. 23, at Hard Rock Rockford’s website or on the Ticketmaster website. 
Earlier this year, Swims released the deluxe edition of his debut album “I’ve Tried Everything but Therapy (Part 1.5).” It includes four brand new songs, one being his smash hit “Lose Control.” 
Swims got his start posting covers online in 2019- such as Michael Jacksons “Rock with You” and Shania Twain’s “You’re Still the One.” He released EPs between 2021 and 2022 such as “Unlearning,” “Tough Love” and “Sleep is Exhausting.” Swims has also collaborated with artists such as Meghan Trainor, X Ambassadors, ILLENIUM and Tiesto. 
The event is one of 10 events announced for Hard Rock Live, the 2000-seat concert venue inside Rockford’s new casino. The casino is set to open August 29.
