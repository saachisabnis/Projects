ROCKFORD, Ill. (WTVO) — Hard Rock Casino Rockford has announced singer-songwriter Teddy Swims will be performing in September.
Swims gained a spot on the Billboard Top 100 for his 2023 single “Lose Control” and again for its follow up, “The Door.”
The singer will perform with Freak Feely at Hard Rock Live, located within the new casino, at 7801 E. State Street, on Friday, September 20th, at 7 p.m. Rockford native DeAndre Hemby will be on drums as part of the “I’ve Tried Everything but Therapy” tour.
Tickets go on sale Friday, August 23rd at 10 a.m.
The new casino opens Thursday, August 29th with an “invitation only” performance by The Nielsen Trust. Akon, Joan Jett and the Blackhearts, and Brad Paisley will each perform on opening weekend.
Swims joins several other announced acts for the upcoming months that include Gipsy Kings, Brian McKnight, and The Black Crowes.
