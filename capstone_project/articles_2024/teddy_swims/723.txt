Get Ready to Ring in 2025 with ‘Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve’ with Ryan Seacrest!
New Year’s Eve is creeping up fast, and it wouldn’t feel complete without ‘Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve’ with Ryan Seacrest! This year’s event promises a star studded musical lineup and midnight magic, broadcasting live on ABC starting at 8 p.m. EST on Dec. 31. Whether you're cozying up at home or hosting a glittery NYE bash, this dazzling show will keep the energy soaring into the early hours of 2025. Not staying up late? No worries—the show will be available to stream on Hulu starting Jan. 1.
Here’s what to look forward to:
🌟 Times Square Magic
Carrie Underwood will be lighting up the Manhattan stage with a medley of her biggest hits, celebrating 20 years since her game-changing American Idol win. And speaking of throwbacks, Sophie Ellis-Bextor is bringing her viral 2001 banger “Murder on the Dancefloor” to the Times Square party.
Legendary girl group TLC will also be dancin’ under the iconic ball drop, alongside country breakout Megan Moroney, who’s fresh from her CMA Awards win. Lenny Kravitz, hot off his latest album Blue Electric Light, will also take the stage, alongside the soulful Teddy Swims, pop sensation Tinashe, and country stars Cody Johnson and Thomas Rhett.
✨ Live from the West Coast 
The party extends to the West Coast with performances from Alanis Morissette, Reneé Rapp, Kesha, Natasha Bedingfield, and T-Pain. Expect an unforgettable duet from Morissette and Rapp that’ll have everyone buzzing! 
🎸 Live from Las Vegas & Puerto Rico
Blake Shelton will check in from Las Vegas, giving fans a country flair to kick off the year. Meanwhile, Luis Fonsi will bring tropical warmth with a performance straight from Puerto Rico. And for a Puerto Rican twist on the evening, co-host Dayanara Torres joins the fun alongside Rita Ora and Ryan Seacrest.
Grab your confetti, pour some bubbly, and get ready to groove into 2025 with this epic lineup. It’s going to be a night to remember! 🕺💃🎆
