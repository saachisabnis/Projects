The Weeknd is giving back!
According to Variety, the "Die for You" singer recently made a donation to "School on Wheels" which supports over 1000 homeless children from Kindergarten through 12th grade!
They note that the donation will go toward things like in-person tutoring, providing laptops and internet access.
