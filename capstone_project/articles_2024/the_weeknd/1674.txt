The Weeknd and Playboi Carti are debuting at No. 3 on this week’s Billboard Hot 100 for their latest collaborative track, “Timeless.”
The collab, which is set to join The Weeknd’s upcoming album Hurry Up Tomorrow, opens with 28.6 million streams, 5.1 million in airplay audience and 6,000 units sold in its first week. In addition to debuting at No. 3 on the Hot 100, the track also hit No. 1 on the Streaming Songs, Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs and Hot R&B Songs charts. “Timeless” gives The Weeknd his highest career bow and his 19th top 10 entry, while this marks Carti’s fifth top 10 on the Hot 100, his second chart-topper on Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs and his first on Hot R&B Songs.
“Timeless” was first debuted live during The Weeknd’s one-off show in Brazil in early September. His upcoming album, Hurry Up Tomorrow, is billed as the culmination of his album trilogy that encompasses After Hours and Dawn FM. A release date for the record is yet to be announced.
