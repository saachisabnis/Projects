WWE took to Twitter today to announce that The Weeknd will be providing the official theme song for this year’s WrestleMania 40 pay-per-view event. This will mark his fifth time being the artist behind the WrestleMania theme. The song is titled “Gasoline”, from his album Dawn FM.
With #WrestleMania season right around the corner, @TheWeeknd becomes the only artist in WWE history to have 5 Official Theme Songs 5 years in a row. “Gasoline” from the album Dawn FM is an Official Theme Song of #WrestleMania XL! pic.twitter.com/pgdulRmvZX
