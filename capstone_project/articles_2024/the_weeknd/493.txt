It’s here again! Here’s the Coachella Schedule for 2024 and when you can stream your favorite acts when they take the stage in the desert. Arguably the most famous music festival in the world, the fun doesn’t stop at Empire Polo Club in Indio, CA—you can watch it from anywhere in the world!
Spanning over two weekends in April, Coachella puts on monumental headlining acts like Harry Styles, Billie Eilish, The Weeknd, and Beyoncé. The festival is run by Goldenvoice who announced this year’s lineup on social media on January 10, 2024. The festival is packed with artists who cover so many genres and perform in the heat of the desert. 2023’s lineup included Gorillaz, Björk, Rosalia, Four Tet, Skrillex, and Fred Again.., and Bad Bunny.
So what’s the Coachella schedule for April 19-21, 2024? Read more to find out. 
The Coachella Headliners are Lana Del Rey, Tyler, The Creator, and Doja Cat. No Doubt will also perform as a special reunion. All four headliners will perform on the Coachella Stage.
