FANS of a critically acclaimed pop-rock singer will be able to enjoy her music brought to life at a Colchester venue early next year.


  The Olivia Rodrigo Experience tribute act will perform at Charter Hall, in Cowdray Avenue, on February 23.


  Olivia Rodrigo has become a globally recognised musician over the last few years.


  You can expect the tribute act to perform many of her big songs like Drivers License, Good 4 U, Get Him Back, Vampire, and Bad Idea Right?.


  Tickets cost £19.95.


  To find out more, visit colchester-events.co.uk/event/the-olivia-rodrigo-experience/.
