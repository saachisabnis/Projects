After ceding the throne to Noah Kahan’s “Stick Season” last week, Benson Boone’s “Beautiful Things” reclaims the #1 position on this week’s Mediabase hot adult contemporary radio chart.
Up one place from last week’s position, the megahit earns a sixth overall week at #1.
“Beautiful Things” received ~5,585 spins during the May 19-25 tracking period, besting last week’s sum by 77.
Teddy Swims’ “Lose Control” rises one spot to #3, while the aforementioned “Stick Season” drops to #3 on the new chart.
Taylor Swift’s “Fortnight (featuring Post Malone)” rises one level to #4, and Sabrina Carpenter’s “Feather” moves down a place to #5.
