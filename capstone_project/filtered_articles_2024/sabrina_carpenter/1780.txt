Consistent with Headline Planet’s projection, Sabrina Carpenter’s “Espresso” and Olivia Rodrigo’s “obsessed” officially enter the Top 10 on this week’s Mediabase pop radio airplay chart.
Played ~9,640 times during the May 19-25 tracking period, “Espresso” rises three spots to #9 on this week’s chart. The count reflects a week-over-week increase of 1,019, which ranks as the pop format’s #6 airplay gain for the week.
Up one spot, “obsessed” secures #10 on this week’s edition of the Mediabase pop chart. The “GUTS (spilled)” deluxe track received ~9,114 spins during the official tracking week (+266).
