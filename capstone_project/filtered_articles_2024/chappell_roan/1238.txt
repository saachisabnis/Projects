Every Friday, Brixton Buzz hosts the popular MegaPop night at the Effra Social in Brixton, with the event running from 10pm to 1am –  and it’s free admission all night!
As ever, you can expect a big, free night of fun, with DJs throwing down huge party tunes from the likes of Taylor Swift, ABBA, Sugababes, Chappell Roan and Fleetwood Mac, plus disco, Britpop, 80s, 90s and 00s and more!
Here’s some photos from last week’s show – we hope to see you on the dancefloor this Friday!
