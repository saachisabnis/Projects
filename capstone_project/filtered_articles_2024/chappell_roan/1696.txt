As summer fades and autumn begins to settle in, music lovers from all over the world will set their sights on Austin, Texas, for the annual Austin City Limits (ACL) Festival. Set to take place over two weekends, October 4-6 and October 11-13, this year’s festival promises to be one of the most memorable yet, featuring an eclectic mix of genres, iconic headliners, and emerging artists.
The 2024 ACL lineup is packed with some of the biggest names in music, ensuring an unforgettable experience for attendees. Headlining this year's festival are:
Tyler, The Creator: Renowned for his genre-blending artistry and electrifying stage presence (see his 2024 Coachella performance), Tyler, The Creator offers a captivating experience with his boundary-pushing music and visually stunning performances.
Dua Lipa: With her chart-topping hits and infectious dance-pop anthems, Dua Lipa brings high-energy performances that get the crowd moving and singing along to every word. As this is her only US date in 2024, expect massive attendance.
Chris Stapleton: As a powerhouse vocalist and masterful songwriter, Stapleton's soulful blend of country, blues, and rock delivers emotionally charged performances that resonate deeply with festival-goers.
Blink-182: Iconic for their high-energy punk rock sound and nostalgic hits, Blink-182’s performances are a thrilling ride through the band’s extensive catalog, promising a lively and unforgettable festival experience.
Pretty Lights: Known for his innovative fusion of electronic music with organic elements and stunning visual productions, Pretty Lights creates immersive live shows that are both visually and sonically mesmerizing.
Sturgill Simpson: With his genre-defying approach to country music and powerful, gritty vocals, his performances are a compelling blend of traditional and modern sounds that captivate audiences.
In addition to these headliners, the festival will feature performances from a diverse array of artists, including:
Porter Robinson: Known for his emotive and cinematic electronic music, Robinson's live shows are a mesmerizing journey through his deeply personal and visually captivating soundscapes.
Fletcher: With her bold, honest lyrics and powerful pop melodies, Fletcher delivers high-energy performances that resonate with authenticity.
Royel Otis: This on-the-rise indie pop duo brings infectious energy and catchy tunes, making their performances a joyous and engaging experience for festival-goers looking for fresh, feel-good music.
Teddy Swims: Swims’ soulful voice and heartfelt performances blend genres seamlessly, offering a raw and emotionally charged experience that deeply connects with the audience.
Chappell Roan: Known for her theatrical pop style and dynamic stage presence, Chappell Roan's performances are vibrant and captivating, leaving festival-goers both entertained and inspired. Her recent stratospheric rise means that you’ll want to arrive early to her set to secure your spot.
Still Woozy: With his laid-back, dreamy sound and playful live performances, Still Woozy creates an atmosphere of relaxed euphoria that is perfect for festival vibes.
While the music is undoubtedly the main attraction, ACL offers so much more. The festival’s location in Zilker Park provides a picturesque backdrop, and attendees can enjoy a variety of local food vendors, craft beer, and unique art installations.
You will also be able to enter the world of Bonus Tracks, the biggest party on the smallest stage at Zilker Park. Programming at Bonus Tracks creates a hub of culture, connection, and positive vibes between music performances. Past appearances include Brené Brown leading thought-provoking conversations with guests Dave Grohl and Brett Goldstein, chats with Billie Eilish and Noah Cyrus, dance parties with Outdoor Voices and Bob’s Dance Shop, and the best of Austin Drag. Stay tuned for the 2024 programming.
Tickets for the Austin City Limits 2024 Festival are on sale now, and some packages are already sold out. Options include single-day passes, weekend passes, and VIP packages that offer exclusive viewing areas, premium amenities, and more. For more information and to purchase tickets, visit the official Austin City Limits Festival website.
Whether you're a longtime attendee or a first-timer, the Austin City Limits 2024 Festival promises an unforgettable experience filled with incredible music, vibrant culture, and the unique spirit of Austin. Don't miss your chance to be part of this iconic event!

On June 24, Round Records & ATO Records will release GarciaLive Volume Six: July 5, 1973 – Jerry Garcia & Merl Saunders, the latest installment of the celebrated GarciaLive archival series. The three-CD set was recorded at the 200 capacity Lion’s Share club formerly located in the small town of San Anselmo, CA, just 20 miles north of San Francisco. The performance features Jerry Garcia performing with friend, mentor and legendary keyboardist/vocalist Merl Saunders. The duo is joined by drummer Bill Vitt and bassist John Kahn, who soon became a lifelong Garcia collaborator.
