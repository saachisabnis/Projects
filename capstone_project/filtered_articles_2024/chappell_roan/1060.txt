Chappell Roan was the musical guest on Saturday Night Live last night, and she did “Pink Pony Club” and a new country song called “The Giver.” Watch her sing the songs below, and scroll down for her promotional video with host John Mulaney and cast member Ego Nwodim. Scroll down for another promotional video that parodies Bob Dylan.
Last night’s episode of Saturday Night Live was the final one before Election Day (November 5), and it featured an appearance from Kamala Harris. The vice president acted alongside Maya Rudolph, who was in character as the Democratic candidate for president. That sketch is below, too.
In 2016 and 2020, Dave Chappelle hosted the first post-election episodes of Saturday Night Live, but the NBC program has tapped a new comedian for November 9, 2024: Bill Burr, who’ll be joined by musical guest Mk.gee. The next week will feature host and musical guest Charli XCX.
