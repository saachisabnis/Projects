According to a report from Fightful Select, Megan Bayne is currently a free agent.
Bayne, who has been away from AEW for quite some time, is currently a free agent and can appear anywhere she wants. Fightful Select is reporting that despite this, creative pitches have been made for her. It is said that Bayne and AEW remain in talks.
Max Caster recently responded to a video of rapper Kanye West performing at a joshi show, and he took to Twitter to ask AEW President Tony Khan to book a match featuring The Acclaimed and Kanye West vs. The Elite.
West performed with Ty Dolla $ign at the Alexander Wang-organized TJPW show over the weekend.
Caster wrote, “Acclaimed, Billy Gunn, and Ye vs the Elite. @TonyKhan book this please!”
