The iHeartRadio Music Festival, now in its 14th year, is set to return to Las Vegas for an exciting two-day event on September 20 and 21. Hosted at the T-Mobile Arena, this festival promises an electrifying, star-studded lineup of artists spanning various genres.
Over these two nights, attendees will enjoy performances from top artists, including A$AP Rocky, The Weeknd, Camila Cabello, Doja Cat, Dua Lipa, Coldplay’s Chris Martin and many more. Ryan Seacrest will host the festival, which will feature must-see collaborations and surprise guests. See the full lineup and how to get tickets below.
General admission single-day and two-day passes can be purchased online through AXS or secondary market platforms such as Vivid Seats, SeatGeek and StubHub.
