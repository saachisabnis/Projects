Girls love karaoke, as does Grammy Award winner Rihanna, who recently gave a surprise performance of her hit singles during an event in New York City over the weekend.
According to several clips circulating social media, the 36-year-old superstar, dressed in an oversized graphic T-shirt, jeans, Bottega Veneta boots, and a large fur hat, jumped onstage on Sunday (Dec 29) during the final invite-only “Girls Love Karaoke” event of the year. 
RELATED: Rihanna Steals the Show at Mariah Carey’s Tour Finale with Unexpected Request
The singer performed “Needed Me” and “Sex with Me” from her eighth and final album, “Anti.” At one point, she was seen sharing the mic and singing with the attendees. 
The Fenty Beauty mogul’s last full concert took place at a pre-wedding party in Jamnagar, India, on March 1, 2024. Before that, she headlined the halftime show in Glendale, Arizona, on February 12, 2023.
