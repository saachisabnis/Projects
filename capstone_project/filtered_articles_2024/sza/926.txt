According to Pitch Fork, Kendrick Lamar and SZA announced their upcoming Grand National Tour. Starting in April they will kick off their North American Tour together and it will end in June. This will not be the first time the two will be performing on tour together, in 2018, they performed at Top Dawg Entertainment Championship Tour. Sza is featured on two songs on Lamar's new album GNX, "Luther" and "Gloria." His album has now reached No.1 on Billboards Hot 100. 
Tickets for this event goes on sale on Friday, December 6th, at 10a.m. local time and a pre-sale begging on Wednesday, December 4th, at 10 a.m. for Cash App card users.
