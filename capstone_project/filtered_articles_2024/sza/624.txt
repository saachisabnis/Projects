May 25 (UPI) -- Pop music star Taylor Swift's Tortured Poets Department is the No. 1 album in the United States for a fourth week.
Coming in at No. 2 on the Billboard 200 chart dated Saturday is Gunna's One of Wun, followed by Morgan Wallen's One Thing at a Time at No. 3, Future and Metro Boomin's We Don't Trust You at No. 4 and Wallen's Dangerous: The Double Album at No. 5.

Rounding out the top tier are Nick Kahan's Stick Season at No. 6, SZA's SOS at No. 7, Zach Bryan's self-titled album at No. 8, Benson & Boone's Fireworks & Rollerblades at No. 9 and Beyonce's Cowboy Carter at No. 10.
