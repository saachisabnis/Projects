It’s just one of them days, no Monica…
The first trailer for “One of Them Days” has been released! The highly anticipated buddy comedy film starring Keke Palmer and SZA is produced by Issa Rae’s HOORAE alongside ColorCreative and MACRO Film Studios. The film, set for release by Sony’s TriStar, is directed by Lawrence Lamont and written by Syreeta Singleton, show-runner of the Rae-produced Max series “Rap Sh!t”.
This film marks SZA’s major acting debut, following her appearance as the musical guest alongside Palmer’s hosting gig on SNL in December 2022.
RELATED: Keke Palmer & SZA Will Star In Buddy Comedy Produced By Issa Rae
Here’s the film’s official summary:
Best friends and roommates Dreux (Keke Palmer) and Alyssa (SZA) are about to have One of Them Days. When they discover Alyssa’s boyfriend has blown their rent money, the duo finds themselves going to extremes in a comical race against the clock to avoid eviction and keep their friendship intact.
The film is also packed with an impressive supporting cast— featuring Lil Rel Howery, Janelle James, and Katt Williams, and more.
“One of Them Days” will hit theaters January 24, 2025. Watch the trailer below!
