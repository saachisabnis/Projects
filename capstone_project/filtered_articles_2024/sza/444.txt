SZA has been announced as one of the headliners of Lollapalooza 2024 in Chicago.Photo by Matt Smith | The Star-Ledger
CHICAGO - The lineup has been announced for one of the biggest music festivals of the year in the country. Among those headlining Lollapalooza in Chicago will be SZA, Blink-182, The Killers and Tyler, The Creator.
And that’s just beginning to scratch the surface as dozens and dozens of singers and bands will perform at the four-day festival being held in Chicago’s Grant Park. In total, there will be more than 150 acts performing on eight different stages.
Tickets can be purchased here. The presale begins on Thursday, March 21st at 10 a.m. CT. Prices increase at noon CT on Thursday when the public on-sale begins.
Other notable performers will be Future X, Metro Boomin, Hozier, Stray Kids, Melanie Martinez, Skrillex, Deftones and Ke$ha. You can see the full lineup of artists here.
