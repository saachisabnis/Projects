Ye, formerly Kanye West, is under investigation by the LAPD after a man claims the mogul punched him in the face on Tuesday evening (April 16) – according to TMZ.  The police report claims that Ye struck the man because he allegedly grabbed or pushed Ye’s wife, Bianca Censori. 
A post shared by Astrowaves (@astrowavess)
According to the report, Ye and his wife left the scene, and the man didn’t require medical treatment. Police plan to speak with the mogul, his wife, and witnesses on the alleged assault. 
Ye and his team have not responded to press inquiries. 
On Tuesday, the couple spent the day at Disneyland in Anaheim, California, where they attracted a lot of attention from fans and paparazzi. They were joined by recording artist Yung Lean.
Ye has a prior history of battery claims. In 2023, he was investigated for throwing a photographer’s phone into an intersection. 
In 2022, Ye was sued by a man who claimed the mogul assaulted him in Downtown Los Angeles. The results of the 2022 lawsuit are unknown. 
The Vultures trilogy’s second installment with Ye/Ty Dolla $ign has been postponed, leaving fans waiting for its release. Volume 2’s cover art was released on March 11. Vultures Vol.1 features the hit songs “Carnivale,” “Back To Me,” and “Talking.”
North West, daughter of Kanye West and Kim Kardashian, announced that she will release her debut album, Elementary School Dropout, in 2024. The album pays homage to her father’s 2004 debut, The College Dropout.
In other news, Ye claimed he washed Kendrick  Lamar on their 2016 collab and Drake at their Hollywood Bowl benefit concert. The feud between Drake and Ye can be traced back to 2010.
