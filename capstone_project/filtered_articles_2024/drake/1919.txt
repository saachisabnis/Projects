NASHVILLE, Tenn. (WSMV) - The search continues Tuesday for a missing and endangered teenager in Sumner County.
Sebastian Wayne Drake Rogers, 15, is missing from the Beech area of Hendersonville, and was last seen Sunday in the area of Beech and Shackle Island, near Long Hollow Pike.
The Tennessee Bureau of Investigation initially issued an Endangered Child Alert over his disappearance, as Rogers has a form of autism. On Tuesday, TBI issued an Amber Alert for Rogers based on “additional investigative information.”
