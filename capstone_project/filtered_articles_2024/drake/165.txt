Drake celebrated three living generations of the Graham family in a touching Father’s Day post.
Drake posted a number of pictures with his son Adonis and father Dennis Graham on Sunday (June 16) as they enjoyed the countryside and rode dirt bikes that were custom built for the rap superstar.

The trio were also sharing a ranch theme with Adonis and Dennis both wearing cowboy hats.
Drake also wrote in the caption: “Three generations [love heart emoji]. Happy Fathers Day to the ones guiding their daughters and sons.”
Drake’s dad recently involved himself in his son’s feud with Kendrick Lamar, Future and Metro Boomin.
Following the release of “Like That” which kickstarted the heated beef, Dennis sarcastically hit out at Drake’s rivals.
