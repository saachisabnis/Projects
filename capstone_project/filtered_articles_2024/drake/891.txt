CAPRON, Va. (WAVY) – Southampton County Deputies arrested a man on Friday for alleged possession of child sexual abuse images.
According to officials, The Southampton County Sheriff’s Office received a tip from both the Southern Virginia Internet Crimes Against Children Task Force and the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children concerning a possible possession of the images.
Detectives were able to execute a search warrant on Jan. 25 at a residence in the 19000 block of Southampton Parkway.
An investigation resulted in the arrest of 30-year-old Cody Drake, who was charged with one count of possession of child pornography and two counts of possession of child pornography second or subsequent offense.
Drake’s court appearance is scheduled for March 26 at the Southampton County General District Court.
