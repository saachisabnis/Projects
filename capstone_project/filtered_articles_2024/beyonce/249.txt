Posted by Ally Reid at Sunday, December 1, 2024 at 10:14 PM
    
Beyoncé has announced the release of a special edition Cowboy Carter art book featuring never-before-seen imagery inspired by her album. Priced at $74, the book contains 136 high gloss pages and is available for preorder on her website. However, the book can only be shipped to US addresses and there is a limit of ten books per customer. The book, set to ship on December 13, has sparked anticipation among fans who were captivated by Beyoncé's Cowboy Carter artwork.
