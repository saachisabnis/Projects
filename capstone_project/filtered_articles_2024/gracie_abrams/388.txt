Gracie Abrams has announced a 2025 European and UK leg of “The Secret of Us Tour” in support of her sophomore album of the same name.
The 19-stop tour will kick off on February 8 in Madrid, Spain, and will include stops in Portugal, Germany, Netherlands, Switzerland, Italy, France, Belgium, the UK, and Ireland before concluding on March 12 in Glasglow, UK.
This is Abrams’ second international headlining tour, following her 2023-2024 “Good Riddance Tour,” which was in support of her debut studio album of the same name.
The “Close To You” singer previously opened for Olivia Rodirgo’s “Sour Tour” and Taylor Swift’s “Eras Tour.” Between her tour dates, Abrams will again join Swift for the final US leg of the “Eras Tour.”
If you’re one of many fans looking forward to hearing favorites from the album live on tour, including “Risk,” “I Love You, I’m Sorry,” “Close To You” and “Us,” featuring Swift, here’s how you can find the cheapest tickets.
Limited tickets are on sale now through Live Nation for select shows, although London, Manchester and Cardiff are already sold out. 
Fans may have better luck with pricing and availability through secondary ticket market platforms like StubHub and TicketCity.
