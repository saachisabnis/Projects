CHICAGO - After nearly two years of performances, Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour concluded Sunday night in Vancouver. 
The tour, which kicked off on March 17, 2023, in Glendale, Arizona, has captivated millions of fans across the globe.
Over the course of 21 months, the Eras Tour celebrated Taylor Swift's musical evolution, featuring hits from every chapter of her iconic career.
