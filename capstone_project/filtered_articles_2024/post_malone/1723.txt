NFL teams went all out to unveil their 2024 schedules in creative ways, featuring appearances from celebrities like Lil Wayne, Post Malone, and Chris Pratt. The Los Angeles Chargers received praise for their video parodying The Sims video game, while the Dallas Cowboys showcased star power with appearances from Post Malone, Saweetie, Jerry Jones, and Michael Irvin. The Green Bay Packers enlisted Lil Wayne, wrestler Braun Strowman, and Joey Graziadei from The Bachelor for their video. The clips were released alongside the NFL schedules for next season and garnered serious attention on social media. (UPI)
