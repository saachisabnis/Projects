Bad Bunny’s next album is set to drop early in January.  The rapper and sometime-WWE star released a new trailer on Thursday announcing that his next album will be Debí Tirar Más Fotos (aka “I Should’ve Taken More Photos”) on January 5th.
The new album, which will be 17 songs, is his first since Nadie Sabe Lo Que Va a Pasar Mañana released in October of 2023.  Bunny also released a new single, “Pitorro De Coco,” which you can check out below.
