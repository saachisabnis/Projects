Home » Bad Bunny Has Been Cast In Darren Aronofsky’s Next Film, ‘Caught Stealing’
According to industry reports, global music superstar Bad Bunny has been cast in a role in the upcoming film from Darren Aronofsky entitled, Caught Stealing. The Grammy-winning musician will join Austin Butler in the cast for Aronofsky’s forthcoming crime thriller for Sony Pictures, which is based on the book by Charlie Huston. The script will be written by Huston as well, with Protozoa producing the picture. While it remains unclear what role Benito will have in the film, Caught Stealing is described as, “A tale following main character Hank Thompson (played by Austin Butler), a burned-out former baseball player, as he’s unwittingly plunged into a wild fight for survival in the downtown criminal underworld of ‘90s New York City.” Other cast members who will be joining Butler and Bad Bunny in Aronofsky’s upcoming film include Zoë Kravitz, Regina King, Matt Smith, Liev Schreiber, and Will Brill.
