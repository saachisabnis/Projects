A host of popular titles are vanishing from the service
Netflix is removing a large number of films and TV shows from its service this April – you can find the full list of titles below.
As well as adding a wide selection of new titles, the UK and US versions of the streaming platform will be removing some of their most popular movies and series. Many of these have already left the service’s library, but you still have week to stream the rest before they disappear.
Among the titles set to leave Netflix are Robert Pattinson’s The Batman, as well as the hit sitcom Community, which has been a regular fixture on the platform many years.
