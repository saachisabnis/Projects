In a monumental moment for hip-hop enthusiasts worldwide, the highly anticipated joint album, ‘WE DON’T TRUST YOU’, by Future and Metro Boomin has finally descended upon us. This 17-track masterpiece is a collaboration that promises to be nothing short of extraordinary, featuring some of the biggest names in the industry including Travis Scott, Playboi Carti, Kendrick Lamar, and Rick Ross.
Clocking in at just over an hour, ‘WE DON’T TRUST YOU’ is a sonic journey that showcases the unparalleled chemistry between Future and Metro Boomin. The album’s tracks were teased at Rolling Loud California, leaving fans eagerly awaiting its official release. Now, the wait is over, and listeners can immerse themselves in the seamless blend of Future’s captivating vocals and Metro Boomin’s innovative production.
One of the standout tracks, “Cinderella,” features the dynamic duo of Future and Travis Scott, delivering a mesmerizing collaboration that is bound to leave a lasting impression. Similarly, “Type Shit” sees Playboi Carti joining forces with Future over Metro Boomin’s infectious beats, resulting in a track that exudes undeniable energy and charisma.
What sets ‘WE DON’T TRUST YOU’ apart is the synergy between Future and Metro Boomin. Their ability to complement each other’s strengths while pushing the boundaries of their individual styles is evident throughout the album. Future’s signature cool and collected delivery effortlessly intertwines with Metro Boomin’s stellar production, creating an immersive listening experience that captivates from start to finish.
It’s no surprise that Future and Metro Boomin are regarded as one of modern hip-hop’s favorite duos. While it may have taken them some time to bless fans with a collaborative project, ‘WE DON’T TRUST YOU’ proves that the wait was undoubtedly worth it. With each track offering something unique, the album solidifies Future and Metro Boomin’s status as trailblazers in the genre.
For fans eager to dive into this musical journey, ‘WE DON’T TRUST YOU’ is now available for streaming on Apple Music.
