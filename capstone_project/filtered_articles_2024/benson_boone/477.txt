Pop radio programmers continue to say yes to Justin Timberlake’s “No Angels,” earning the song this week’s most added honor.
The new single won support from 27 Mediabase-monitored pop stations, yielding a narrow first-place finish on the add board.
With 26 adds each, Sabrina Carpenter’s “Espresso,” Hozier’s “Too Sweet,” and Benson Boone’s “Slow It Down” tie for second.
With 24 adds, Doja Cat’s “OKLOSER” grabs fifth place on the Mediabase pop add board.
This week’s other notable pop radio options: d4vd’s “Feel It” (19 adds, 6th-most), Dasha’s “Austin” (17 adds, 7th-most), Chappell Roan’s “Good Luck, Babe!” (16 adds, 8th-most), Muni Long’s “Made For Me” (15 adds, 9th-most), and Beyonce & Miley Cyrus’ “II MOST WANTED” (14 adds, 10th-most).
