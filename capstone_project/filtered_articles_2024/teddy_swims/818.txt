The annual iHeart Radio Jingle Ball Tour includes a show in Phila.
The Q102 Jingle Ball will be held at 7:30 p.m. on Monday, Dec. 16, 2024, at the Wells Fargo Center. Featured performers include Katy Perry, Shaboozey, Gracie Abrams, Meghan Trainor, Tate McRae, Teddy Swims, Jason Derulo and Dasha.
