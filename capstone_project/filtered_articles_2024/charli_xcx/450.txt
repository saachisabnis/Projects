Shygirl and Saweetie have shared the studio version of “Immaculate” after debuting the song on Charli XCX and Troye Sivan’s Sweat Tour. Check it out below.
Shygirl, who has released the Club Shy and Club Shy (Rmx) EPs this year, will rejoin Charli XCX on tour in the United Kingdom next month, having featured on the “365” remix on Brat and It’s Completely Different but Also Still Brat.
