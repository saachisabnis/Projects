Future, Metro Boomin & Kendrick Lamar’s “Like That,” which helped propel an unforgettable chapter in hip-hop earlier this year, has officially earned #1 on a key radio chart.
Up two places from last week’s position, “Like That” secures #1 on this week’s Mediabase rhythmic radio listing.
The song received ~6,316 spins during the May 5-11 tracking period, topping last week’s count by 800.
SZA’s “Saturn” holds at #2, while Flo Milli’s “Never Lose Me (featuring Lil Yachty)” descends from #1 to #3.
4Batz’s “act ii: date @ 8” rises one place to #4 on this week’s rhythmic chart, and 21 Savage’s “redrum” drops a level to #5.
