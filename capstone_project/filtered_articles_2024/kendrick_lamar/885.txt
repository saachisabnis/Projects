GloRilla’s “Yeah Glo!” completes its ascent to #1 on this week’s Mediabase urban radio chart.
Played ~7,465 times during the May 12-18 tracking period, “Yeah Glo!” rises one place to the summit. This week’s spin count bests last week’s mark by a sizable 917 plays.
Up one place, Future, Metro Boomin & Kendrick Lamar’s “Like That” earns #2. Cardi B’s “Enough (Miami)” concurrently ascends three levels to #3.
BossMan Dlow’s “Get In With Me,” last week’s leader, falls to #4 on this week’s chart. Sexyy Red’s “Get It Sexyy” concurrently holds at #5.
