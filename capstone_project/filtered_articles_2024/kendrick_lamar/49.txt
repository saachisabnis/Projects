Kendrick Lamar‘s creative range goes far beyond rap and music, and his latest collaboration with one of the world’s best-known luxury fashion houses is evidence of that.
On Tuesday (January 23), Chanel premiered its Spring-Summer 2024 Haute Couture Show. The set, which featured an enormous jewel button with the company logo hovering over the circular walkway, was designed by the Compton native along with Dave Free and Mike Carson.

“At the invitation of Virginie Viard, Kendrick Lamar and creative partner Dave Free meet the world of CHANEL Haute Couture for the first time, bringing to life a story about time and transmission,” a statement by pgLang said about the video.
