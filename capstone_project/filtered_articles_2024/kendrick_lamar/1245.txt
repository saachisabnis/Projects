Travis Scott and Mustard have offered fans a preview of their upcoming collaboration “Parking Lot.”
The producer uploaded a clip to his Instagram on Tuesday (June 18) of them working together in the studio, with the track being featured throughout the video.
AD LOADING...
At the end of the short clip, a graphic flashes up revealing the release date for the single as Friday (June 21).
The song will be featured on Mustard’s new album Faith of a Mustard Seed, which is set to drop on July 26.
Faith of a Mustard Seed follows 2019’s Perfect 10, which featured the hits “Pure Water” with Migos and “Ballin” with Roddy Ricch.
Travis Scott and Mustard have worked together several times in the past, most notably on the 2016 single “Whole Lotta Lovin'” which was later certified gold.

Mustard has been enjoying a big few months following the success of Kendrick Lamar’sDrake diss track “Not Like Us,” which he produced and went to the top of the Billboard Hot 100.
The song marked the producer’s first time hitting the top of the chart, as the track broke various streaming records at the height of the Kendrick and Drake beef last month.

Mustard also received some love from Kendrick Lamar’s former TDE labelmate ScHoolboy Q, who said that he’d already heard Faith of a Mustard Seed and couldn’t wait for everyone else to check it out.
“Heard it [teary face emoji],” he wrote. “sHit go dumb. LA bout to do it again… LA up tHis year.”
